How are vascular occlusions treated if the patient is highly allergic to Hyaluronidase?
Contraindication to HA fillers
Feb 22, 2023
A responsible injector would not proceed with HA filler injections if hyaluronidase was not available for treatment of a vascular occlusion. Many alternatives to filler exist for rejuvenation that could be an option for a patient with hyaluronidase allergy including fat grafting.

I would not personally lift a facelift flap encompassing a region of current HA filler due to vascularity concerns.

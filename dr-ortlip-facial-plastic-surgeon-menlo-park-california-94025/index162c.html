Can you advise on nose tip swelling 6 months post-op open rhinoplasty?
Tip Swelling
Apr 20, 2023
Nasal tip swelling can be common after rhinoplasty surgery. Especially within the first year. This does not surprise me and I don't think it should concern you much. Laying down allows all the blood to rush into the head/face and soft tissue can tend to swell. Trying to sleep slightly upright or some gentle taping of the tip at night under the advice of your plastic surgeon could potentially help with this. I think with time this will be less of an issue.

What could be done about my chin?
Genioplasty
Mar 7, 2023
Great question, assuming this is our normal anatomy, the only thing that could be done is a genioplasty. This is done through an intra-oral incision. The excess bone of the chin is then cut off and re-positioned to provide a less projected, smoother appearance. This procedure is performed a lot in asian culture as well as for facial feminizations surgery.
Can revision build back my bridge narrow and thin?
Bridge Issues
Mar 7, 2023
The short answer is yes. It can be fixed. A common problem we see is over resected nasal bones and under-refined nasal tips. This leads to the sense that the nose is flat and the tip is over-projected. It is likely that you will need a more extensive procedure including rib cartilage harvest, possible ear cartilage harvest and temporalis fascia. We perform similar procedure on ethnic patients to help build up the nasal dorsum and give more projections. Hope this helps and good luck!

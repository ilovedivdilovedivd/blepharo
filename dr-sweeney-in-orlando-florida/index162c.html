Right implant still not dropping. Will I need a revision?
Breast Revision
May 28, 2021
At this point it is likely a breast revision will be needed. Without proper examination it is hard to pinpoint the exact cause of an implant that has failed to drop. It can be capsular contracture, incomplete release of the pectorals muscle in a dual plane augmentation, fold not lowered enough, and insufficient scoring of the breast parenchyma. Pre-operatively the right side appears to be more constricted so I am sure that played a factor.

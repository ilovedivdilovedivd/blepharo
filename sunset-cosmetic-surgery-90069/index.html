Filler for nasolabial folds
Jan 10, 2019
Hello and thank you for the question. Your folds do not appear to be very deep so you may be a good candidate to have filler placed in this area. It is always a good idea to have an in person consultation for this, as it is helpful to have the injector assess you with animation and natural movement. Please be very careful not to have this area completely filled so there is no fold as this is a very unnatural look.

Should I worry about a little bruising that came up on my lip after Juvederm?
Bruising with lip filler
Dec 10, 2018
Hi,
Bruising can happen with lip filler and is not dependent upon the volume so much as the placement. The bruise can last up to 2 weeks. I'm not sure if you were told, but try to avoid any blood thinners about a week prior to any injection as this could potentially make any bruise a little worse than normal. Arnica and or Bromelain may help. You can also alternate warm and cold compresses to help the bruise dissipate a little more quickly.
Hope this helps!

Should I wait to have an upper blepharoplasty once my brow lift heals? Worried about having too
Timing of blepharoplasty and brow lift
Jun 18, 2019
Typically, it is common to have brow lift and blepharoplasty performed at the same time, with specific measurements taken by the surgeon to ensure too much tissue is not taken. In some circumstances it may be wise to perform one at a time, for example in a patient with severe dry eyes, or who has already had a blepharoplasty with scar tissue, or for cost purposes.

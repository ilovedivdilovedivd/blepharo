bruising after labiaplasty
Oct 10, 2018
Unfortunately, bruising can occur after a labiaplasty procedure. I would encourage you to use cold packs to decrease swelling and provide some comfort. I would minimize anti inflammatories due to the possible risk of increased bleeding. I would also encourage you to minimize activity over the next few days. If things continue to worsen, please see your surgeon sooner than scheduled.

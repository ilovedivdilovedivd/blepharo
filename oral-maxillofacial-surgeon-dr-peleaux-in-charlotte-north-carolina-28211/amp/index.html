What happens if I stop laser hair removal after 1 session?
What happens if laser hair reduction treatments are stopped after one session
Apr 5, 2017
Since only active hair follicles are affected, only a fraction of the total hair follicles are destroyed during any one treatment. Usually 5-8 treatments are necessary to achieve the desired result. You will probably notice only minimal hair reduction after one treatment and it is very unlikely that overgrowth will occur.
Can I shave after laser hair removal on my face, neck & chin with a Men's Norelco Electric Shave
Is shaving okay after Hair reduction treatment
Apr 5, 2017
Mild swelling or redness may occur after Laser hair reduction treatment, but usually resolves in 2-3 days. Shaving should be okay if you have no redness or swelling. Until redness has resolved, it is recommended to avoid applying cosmetics to treated areas. Avene products are excellent for treating post treatment inflammation.

How long does it take to Radiesse to go away from lips?
Radiesse Time in Lips
Jan 28, 2016
I never use Radiesse in the lips. Typically Radiesse lasts approximately 1 year, but because it can be injected in the wrong plane it can last longer. Unlike other fillers, you cannot melt Radiesse, so be patient, and don’t put Radiesse in your lips again.
Can Ultrashape of the abdomen be performed in the presence of a small umbilical hernia?
Umbilical Hernia
Jan 28, 2016
Yes. UltraShape can be performed if the hernia is localized, and the provider doesn’t not treat the area directly. It is important to get in the hands of someone that is very experienced. The UltraShape provider in my office is a national trainer for the device and can handle anything.
FX laser contraindicated when taking Humira?
Humira
Jan 28, 2016
Humira doesn’t inhibit wound healing. Therefore, Fraxel is well tolerated while on Humira. Fraxel is great for clearing blotchy skin as well as improving texture. It’s one of my favorite procedures of all time.
Do I have to use a face wash when I’m on Accutane treatment?
Face wash on accutane
Jan 28, 2016
Yes, when on Accutane, a topical regimen is just as important as oral therapy. It is important to use something that is not too drying – like cetaphil or Cera Ve wash because Accutane can dry your skin. Especially if on a full dose.

How long does it take to heal on the inside after tummy tuck?
How long to heal on inside from Tummy Tuck?
Dec 7, 2014
Thanks for your question.Generally by 3 months you are 70-80% healed (both inside and out). The remaining 20% will take up to 18 months for the scar tissue to mature. This may be further delayed for up to 2 years or more if you have diabetes, hypertension, immunocompromised or a smoker etc.Hope this helps.

Will inner thigh liposuction be suitable for me?
Inner Thigh Liposuction?
Oct 12, 2014
Thanks for your question. In view of your age, you would have good quality skin and liposuction may be quite unforgiving in this region ie. may lead to indentations or corrugations in the skin. I would suggest you consider CoolSculpting by Zeltiq which is an alternative to liposuction but does not require surgery, anaesthesia, downtime and is painless. It has the added advantage of potentially skin shrinkage which would be very beneficial in obtaining a “box gap” to the inner thigh.
Are my breasts too close together?
Do I Have Symmastia?
Oct 8, 2014
Thanks for your question. By definition, you do not have symmastia where the breast implants “kiss” each other in the midline of the chest to form the “figure-of-eight’ or “bread loaf” sign. If your breasts were close together before the breast augmentation, then they will also be close together after the operation. You are also still quite swollen especially in the upper poles of the breasts but this will settle in due course over the next 6-12 weeks. Good luck with it all.
2 weeks post op. Am I healing normally? Is this amount of swelling normal?
Post-op Wound Healing Problem
Oct 8, 2014
Thanks for your question. From looking at your photos, it would appear that you have the onset of a wound infection or wound healing problem. The first thing to do is see your plastic surgeon who may either place you on a course of oral antibiotics or even admit you into hospital for intravenous antibiotics for a few days. An ultrasound to the breast to exclude an underlying fluid collection (either seroma or blood) may also be warranted. Hope it all goes well for you.

Can a Tummy Tuck Be Paid for by OHIP?
Tummy tuck covered by OHIP
Sep 5, 2018
Tummy tuck is considered a cosmetic procedure and is not covered by OHIP. There is a procedure called a panniculectomy which if approved is covered by OHIP. A panniculectomy is excess skin in the pannus area also referred to as an apron, that hangs down over the genitals and or thighs. During your initial consultation a certified plastic surgeon will be able to determine which procedure is best for you.

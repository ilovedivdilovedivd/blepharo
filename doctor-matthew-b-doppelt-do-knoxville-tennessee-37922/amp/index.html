Can I get a hydrafacial if I have mild rosacea?
Hydrafacial and rosacea
Jan 18, 2021
Absolutely! Hydrafacial can be customized in a way as not to aggravate Rosacea. Your aesthetician can determine the best treatment at your consultation. Be sure to tell her all the oral and topical medications you are using as well.

SculpSure v Kybella
Jan 15, 2021
SculpSure would be more effective (to destroy fats and help with skin retraction) than Kybella. Kybella requires multiple sessions of injections with swelling which require social downtime.
If IPL is done prior to Botox, will heat generated in face from IPL-related inflammation (side e
Botox Migration
Jan 15, 2021
I do not do Botox on the same day as any other facial procedures (except filler). Yes, heat and inflammation have the potential to cause botox migration and I never put a needle through skin that has been "damaged" with a laser. To be safe, although it may be a little over cautious, I have patients wait 2 weeks after botox for other procedures and until the skin has completely healed from any lasers if they have done those first to receive botox/filler treatments.
Is Restylane Kysse or Juvederm Ultra better?
Kysse vs Juvederm Ultra
Nov 17, 2020
Both Juvederm and Restylane are Hyaluronic Acid fillers. The difference is in the manufacturing and cross-linking process of the HA. Either product is great for lips. Since Kysse is newer product to the market, injectors are trying it to see how it works. From what we are seeing and hearing in the industry from peers so far is that Kysse doesn't do as well combined with other fillers in the lips. So, it's best for "virgin" lips or for lips that have only been injected with Kysse previously.

Sebastian Dunda, MD

Name: Sebastian Dunda, MD
Statement: DR. MED. SEBASTIAN E. DUNDA is a board certified plastic and aesthetic surgery specialist and active member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS) as well as of the International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ISAPS). Dr. Dunda treats his patients in his private practice in Berlin, situated in the famous and historical Friedrichstraße, close to the Gendarmenmarkt.

DR. MED. SEBASTIAN E. DUNDA has earned an excellent reputation over the years, both in Berlin and out of town, especially in the field of breast surgery. Amongst the procedures he most frequently performs we should mention the breast augmentation with the most modern implants using a tissue-conserving technique (combined or only with the use of the patient’s own fat). With a great deal of experience and thanks to the latest worldwide developments, breast lifting and breast reduction are another essential part of the most popular operations at the Dr. Dunda’s practice. Here, emphasis is placed above all on scar-saving techniques and long-lasting results, which is possible because of the use of the inner bra as well as artificial nets.

Dr. Dunda’s range of services also includes liposuction and body contouring procedures, such as tummy tucks after weight loss or after pregnancy. To make it easier for you to access your desired treatment, Dr. Dunda offers various financing options in cooperation with selected partners.

During a consultation, Dr. Dunda discusses the best possible solution and informs you about the procedure, risks and side effects. In addition, a 3D simulation is also available in almost all cases in order to get an accurate idea of the realistic outcome of a planned procedure.

Dr. Sebastian E. Dunda will be happy to advise you based on his long experience, both in person, in his private practice in Berlin or online via the video consultation.

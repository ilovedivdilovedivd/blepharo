Sanjay Grover, MD, FACS

Name: Sanjay Grover, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Sanjay Grover is a Stanford-trained, board-certified plastic surgeon with over 22 years in practice. He is the founder of Grover Aesthetics in Newport Beach, one of Southern California’s premier plastic surgery destinations.

Dr. Grover was recently selected by Newsweek magazine as one of America’s Best Plastic Surgeons for facelift, rhinoplasty, breast augmentation and liposuction.

Dr. Grover specializes in procedures on the face, nose, breast, and body and is sought after for his expertise in breast enhancement, tummy tucks, complete mommy makeovers, rhinoplasty, and a wide range of other procedures by patients from across the United States and overseas.

Patients will find a surgeon who is compassionate and thoughtful, eager to listen and educate, and uniquely capable of creating beautiful outcomes thanks to his exceptional skill at performing often-delicate, complex surgeries.

Dr. Grover obtained his medical degree from the University of California in San Diego. After graduation, he completed residencies in General Surgery and Plastic Surgery at Stanford University, followed by a fellowship in Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and Oculoplastic Surgery Fellowship at PACES Plastic Surgery and Recovery Center in Atlanta, Georgia.

Board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, Dr. Grover is a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, the American Society for Laser Medicine and Surgery, the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, and the Rhinoplasty Society. He is a fellow of the American College of Surgeons.

Dr. Grover is the recipient of numerous awards, including multiple Castle Connolly’s Top Doctors distinctions, an Orange County Medical Association Physician of Excellence, Orange Coast Magazine’s Top Doctors, Los Angeles Magazine’s SuperDoctors, and a US News & World Report Top Doctor. He is a frequent contributor and expert for major media publications, including Extra, Access Hollywood, BBC, Fox News, Hallmark Channel, and The Doctors.

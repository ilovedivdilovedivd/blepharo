LAT flap breast reconstruction after upper back lift?
Thank you for your question!
Feb 12, 2020
The LAT flap is dependent on perforating vessels through the muscle out to the skin. A torsoplasty may have interrupted these vessels depending on the surgeon and his technique. The short answer is probably not a candidate. 
Can fluid build up from latissimus dorsi clap cause fluid to also build around lung?
Thank you for your question!
Feb 11, 2020
At 5 weeks there is still inflammation around the site of surgery that may cause fluid. But not necessarily because of the implant. I would recommend to follow up with your plastic surgeon about this fluid. 
Is It’s safe to keep my left capsule in, right one was taken out due to Capsulated implant?
Thank you for your question!
Feb 6, 2020
A capsule is an expected reaction of the body to an implant. They are usually removed or altered when you develop a capsular contracture as you described on your right breast. Unless there are any issues with your left side, it should be fine. Make sure you follow up with your plastic surgeon on a regular basis.

Thank you for your question!
Feb 5, 2020
Hyaluronic acid fillers approved by the FDA in the United States to use (Juvederm, Restylane, Bellotero, Versa) do not contain those allergens that you mention.However, if you still have a concern, you should do a skin test in the office prior to injecting your face, just in case. 
How can you remove the "bunched up" skin around the eyes that appear when smiling?
Thank you for your question!
Jan 31, 2020
The answer to your "bunching up" of skin is going to depend on how much skin is bunching. Botox will only relax the muscles around the eyelid that create the crows feet. Laser resurfacing works by tightening the skin and helping to minimize wrinkles by inducing the skin to make a smoother surface. If there is too much skin a lower lid blepharoplasty and/or midfacelift may be the answer. In person consultation is best. 
If I am allergic to Revolax lip fillers can I try other brands?
Thank you for your question!
Jan 28, 2020
Revolax is an inexpensive over the counter filler that is cross linked. It is also not legal to inject this filler in the States as it is not FDA approved. Doing a test of the filler (even elsewhere) can be a good idea. This way you verify that you're not allergic to a specific filler. If you do not have a reaction then you may proceed to have the filler injected at a later day. 
When can you redo filler after having it dissolved?
Thank you for your question!
Jan 27, 2020
You can do filler after having it dissolved. Ideally, you would want to wait 7-10 days to let all swelling subside. If it's only a small area it should be ready after 3 or 5 days.

What do I do about these parentheses?
Fillers, laser or facelift.
Jan 23, 2020
The marionette lines that you are referring to can be improved with filler. Laser can help erase these lines too. A facelift can also help correct these stubborn lines.
Can Voluma in cheeks be melted with a laser?
Probably not.
Jan 23, 2020
Voluma is a highly cross linked filler that is hard to dissolve, dilute or melt with anything else than Hyaluronidase. To my knowledge there are no studies that support melting this filler with laser.
Is it safe to get Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy if I have Restylane and Voluma in my face?
It's safe.
Jan 20, 2020
HBOT is safe and it helps with wound healing in general. Not a contraindication if you have fillers in your face.
Can muscle be trimmed (removed) during a facelift?
In some patients.
Jan 20, 2020
During a face lift the tissues under the skin can be lifted, tightened and trimmed to rejuvenate the cheek and neck and jawline. In some patients when they ave excess muscle in their neck (large bands); some of it can be trimmed or cut to create a nicer jawline and neck contour.
In general, muscle is not removed from the face; but in some cases excess muscle can be removed from lower lids or the neck.
Can electromagnetic stimulation (EMS) cause under eye filler to migrate?
Not to my knowledge.
Jan 20, 2020
EMS should not cause migration of a filler from around the eyelids to another area. But I have seen stranger things happen.

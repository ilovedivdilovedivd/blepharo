Jonathan R. Freed, MD

Name: Jonathan R. Freed, MD
Statement: With nearly three decades of experience, Dr. Jonathan R. Freed, MD is a board-certified plastic surgeon and the founder of Freed Plastic Surgery. He is known for providing beautiful, natural results to patients from the greater Sacramento area and Northern California. Though he performs a wide range of procedures, he is best known for his skill performing mommy makeover surgery, breast enhancement, and weight loss surgery.

Dr. Freed received his MD from Loma Linda University School of Medicine before completing his residency in plastic and reconstructive surgery at that university’s medical center. He is the former chief of plastic surgery at Kaiser Permanente in Portland, Oregon, Sutter Auburn Faith Hospital in Auburn, CA, and Sierra Nevada Memorial Hospital in Grass Valley, CA, as well as a past clinical instructor in plastic surgery at Oregon Health & Science University. Dr. Freed is a member of the prestigious American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery.

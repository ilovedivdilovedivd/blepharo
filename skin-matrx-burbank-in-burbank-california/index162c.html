Doctors

George Orloff, MD, FACS

Name: George Orloff, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. George Orloff has been a plastic surgeon in California for nearly three decades. Certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, he currently practices at Burbank Plastic Surgery, an elite, state-of-the-art medical facility in the San Fernando Valley where he specializes in facial cosmetic surgery. His motto of “patient care and patient results, first” emphasizes the personalized, hands-on approach he takes with every person in his care.

“My promise to you is to provide the very best result in a safe and comfortable environment at the highest level possible,” says Dr. Orloff. “I will stay close and accessible through all stages of your procedure. Making people happier and improving their lives is why I became a surgeon in the first place.”

Dr. Orloff graduated magna cum laude from the University of Southern California, where he was a research fellow in plastic surgery and a resident in plastic and reconstructive surgery. Dr. Orloff completed nine years of additional training after medical school, becoming Chief Resident in general surgery at the University of California, San Francisco. His extensive training culminated in the prestigious Craniofacial Fellowship at UCLA. He later served as the Chief of plastic surgery at Providence St. Joseph Medical Center for many years.

A member of the American Society of Plastic Surgery and Fellow American College of Surgeons, Dr. Orloff has been honored with a number of awards, including the Roche Laboratories Award for Excellence in Basic Science Research and Resident of the Year at San Francisco Kaiser Hospital M.D., UC Irvine. He’s performed more than 11,000 procedures throughout his career.

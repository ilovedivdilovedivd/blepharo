Debra L. Stafford, MD

Name: Debra L. Stafford, MD
Statement: Dr. Stafford embraces modern and innovative surgical techniques, instrumentation and technology to achieve effective and safe results in addition to providing patients as rapid and comfortable recovery as possible. Dr Stafford has extensive experience in body, breast and facial aesthetic/cosmetic surgery. In addition, Dr Stafford has substantial experience in performing liposuction and excision body contouring in awake patients. Dr Stafford works diligently to provide her patients a good experience during their procedures with minimal pain. Her patients also give her high reviews in bedside manner.

Aesthetic medicine can be particularly challenging for the aesthetic/cosmetic surgeon because cosmetic surgery is elective and patients have spent their entire lives looking at their own bodies in conjunction with societal standards and expectations and some will have very specific and detailed visions of how they wish to look. Dr Stafford believes that often the most important thing any physician can do (especially a plastic/cosmetic surgeon) for their patients is to listen. Dr Stafford is very good at connecting to patients and putting them at ease and allaying their fears concerning undergoing a procedure. Furthermore, Dr Stafford has extensive experience in body contouring in all body types including full figured individuals with higher BMIs. Dr Stafford possesses a strong aesthetic eye and works with her patients to develop a customized treatment plan that would fit the patients’ needs, desires and expectations.

In conclusion, Dr. Stafford strives to provide her patients with the most beautiful and natural-looking results possible to improve their self-confidence prioritizing patients’ safety whiles giving expert and compassionate care.

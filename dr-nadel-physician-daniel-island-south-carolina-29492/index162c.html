Can you do honey facial after Botox?
Facial after Botox
Sep 20, 2018
It is okay to get a facial after Botox but I would wait at least a day before doing that. The risk of getting a facial immediately after Botox treatment is that the medicine will move into places where we do not want it to go which can cause complications such as eyelid drooping. If that does happen, there is no reversal agent for Botox, so you would have to wait until the botox wore off.

I'm following a time restricted feeding program. Is it OK for me to drink black coffee during the fast
Coffee during a fast
Sep 12, 2018
There are various ways to do a fast. Some allow bone broth during the fasting period. Other fasts are more restricted where you can only drink water. Most fast, however, allow black coffee as there are no calories in it. Coffee is a diuretic so keep up with your hydration if you are drinking it during a fasting period.

Aug 20, 2018
Hyperpigmentation (when skin become darker in color than the normal) is one of the most commonly encountered complications with chemical peels. There are several causes (inadequate sun protection, aggressive peels, sensitive skin, etc) and it is hard to say what the cause is without knowing more information, like your Fitzpatrick skin type or which chemical peel and strength was used.

Biography: Jennifer Fick, MSN, FNP-C, CANS, began her career in the cosmetic medical industry in 1998, and has been providing laser and injectable treatments in the field of cosmetic dermatology for over 20 years. She began providing aesthetic injections in 2000, beginning with Botox®, and then Collagen in 2001. The approval of Restylane® in 2004 signaled the introduction of cosmetic injections into other areas of the face, including the cheek, tear trough, temple, forehead and hands. She also provides Botox® for therapeutic indications, including hyperhidrosis and migraine treatment. She was involved in the development of the IPL Photofacial treatment therapies in use today.
She graduated from Georgetown University in 2016 as a Family Nurse Practitioner, and is board certified by the American Association of Nurse Practitioners. She is board certified as a Certified Aesthetic Nurse Specialist by the International Society of Plastic and Aesthetic Nurses.

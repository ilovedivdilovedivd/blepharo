Behrad Brad Aynehchi, MD

Name: Behrad Brad Aynehchi, MD
Statement: Dr. Behrad Brad Aynehchi, MD is a leading facial plastic surgeon and the founder of Westside Face with offices in Culver City and Santa Monica, California. Double board certified by the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery, he specializes in closed scarless rhinoplasty, revision rhinoplasty, facelift, and neck lift. Having spent 13 years in practice and performed thousands of successful surgeries, Dr. Aynehchi remains committed to delivering natural-looking results with minimal downtime.

“Undertaking facial surgery takes a tremendous amount of courage,” says Dr. Aynehchi. “As my own harshest critic, I take this responsibility very seriously and never settle for less than an outstanding and personalized result.”

After graduating magna cum laude from the University of California, Berkeley, Dr. Aynehchi received his medical degree at Temple University in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He went on to complete his specialist training at the New York University Grossman School of Medicine in Manhattan.

Currently, Dr. Aynehchi serves as the co-director of Head and Neck Surgery at 90210 Surgery Medical Center and as an instructor for UCLA medical students. He is an active member of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery.

After Botox for masseter muscle wears off, will my jaw return to my pre-injected state?
It will most likely return to normal.
Oct 10, 2019
Thank you for you question,
First, the results you see from Botox are somewhat dependent the current size of you masseter muscles. If you have large masseters from grinding you teeth, you may see more of a decrease in their size. It will often take more than one treatment to see a reduction in size, as the muscles atrophy from lack of use. Secondly, after the Botox wears off and if you are still over using your masseters you will see them return to a large size.
Can I get Botox/Dysport during my period/menstrual cycle?
Yes, it is safe during your period.
Sep 20, 2019
Thank you for submitting this question.
I have been asked this by my patients. The use of BOTOX or other neuromodulators are not contraindicated during your period. The injections could feel slightly more painful, but my patients have not noticed a difference in the minimal discomfort that occurs with the injection. Injection technique will probably be a bigger factor in the amount of discomfort felt.

Should I have a bone graft upon #2 extraction in anticipation of implant years later?
Immediate placement will be better
Apr 19, 2019
Bone grafting at the time of extraction is a much simpler and often a less expensive procedure than trying to gain bone at a later date. On the other side, is the cost of the graft. Implants for tooth #2 can be difficult to restore and many patients do just fine without tooth #2. The decision is yours. I sure you will make the right choice.
Can I eat salted chips after whitening teeth?
Yes, you can eat anything.
Apr 19, 2019
You can anything after whitening. You may want to stay away from foods like red wine, coffee and tea which will stain your teeth again. You may also want to avoid very cold foods, as your teeth may be sensitive to cold foods after a whitening treatment.
Can the “snap on smile” help with widening an already straight smile?
Snap On Smile Can Help
Feb 23, 2018
A ‘Snap On Smile’ is a cosmetic treatment. If your only concern is you want a wider smile, then yes a ‘Snap On Smile’ can help. One must remember that usually it is not functional, meaning you can wear it to obtain a different look with you teeth, but it is not strong enough for eating. They are not a definitive treatment, but a good way of knowing what one’s smile could look like with braces and/or veneers.
Is it legitimate for dentists to require payment in full before beginning months of cosmetic work

While dishing out $30,000 is never fun, it is even less fun for a dentist to do $30,000 of work and not get paid.

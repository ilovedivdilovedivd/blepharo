Does microneedling/PRP plump the face or does it just build collagen that works on the skin
Microneedling with PRP
Jun 29, 2019
The goal of microneedling with PRP treatments is to stimulate collagen growth on the skin in order to improve its overall quality and texture.  It does not make your face plump the way fillers do. Some patients elect to have PRFM (platelet rich fibrin matrix - a more solidified form of PRP) to be injected at the same time and that can be used for volume augmentation.  Goretti H. Taghva MD FACS

What can I do about the filler in my nose glowing red in the sun?
Glow in the sun nose filler
May 1, 2019
Hyaluronic acid is a translucent gel.  When a lot is placed under thin skin, it can appear translucent like what we see on your photos.  If you like the shape of your nose, you can try to see if BB cream/ foundation can do a better job covering it.  If you really can’t stand the look of it, then dissolving it would be a good option.  You may then consider trying a different filler that isn’t HA based, or surgical rhinoplasty. Good luck!Goretti H. Taghva MD FACS

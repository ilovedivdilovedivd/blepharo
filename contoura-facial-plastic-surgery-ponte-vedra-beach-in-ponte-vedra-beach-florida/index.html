Roberto E. Garcia, MD, FACS

Name: Roberto E. Garcia, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Roberto Garcia, MD, FACS is a double-board-certified facial plastic surgeon and the founder of Contoura Facial Plastic Surgery, located in Ponte Vedra Beach, Florida. He has over 15 years of experience and has performed over 15,000 surgeries, specializing in and exclusively performing facial aesthetic and reconstructive procedures. Dr. Garcia’s Northeast Florida patients seek him out for his expertise in performing facelifts, blepharoplasties, and other procedures, but also for his compassionate nature.

He also manages a non-profit organization, F.A.C.E., which provides reconstructive surgeries to children.

Should I wait for lip injections until after rhinoplasty?
Lip filler after Rhinoplasty
Nov 13, 2018
Thank you for your question.  I would highly recommend waiting until after the rhinoplasty before you have any lip injections.  Rhinoplasty will alter the appearance of the lips for quite some time and can give the injector a false appearance.

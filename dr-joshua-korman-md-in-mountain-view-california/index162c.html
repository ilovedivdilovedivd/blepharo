Chin implant versus non-surgical options? I have a weak chin.
Chin implant yes or no
Feb 10, 2021
The fat pocket under the chin is partly a fat issue (more than you want), and skin (too much). A chin implant will help "use up the skin" so that will definitely help reduce the fat pocket under the chin when coupled with liposuction. Ultherapy or other nonsurgical options probably won't do enough to make a significant difference.
Hope that helps!

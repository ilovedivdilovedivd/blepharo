Reappearing mole with pain
Apr 13, 2015
Your initial pain was likely due to the pressure from the mole which can affect the underlying sensory nerves in the scalp. A benign mole such as this not only has a top portion which you can see, but a bottom portion that is present deeper in the skin. When pressed, this bottom portion can push against a nerve and cause pain. Your biopsy procedure was most likely a shave biopsy which removed the top portion and left the bottom portion. Can I dye my hair the day after Botox injections?
Botox and hair dye
Apr 13, 2015
It would be perfectly fine to dye your hair the day after a Botox treatment. Even if you were to develop an allergic reaction to the dye, there would be no effect on your Botox treatment.
Am I suppose to see needle marks by the 3rd day after Juvderm injections?
Treatment for post injection bruising
Apr 13, 2015
Injection marks after filler treatments are common and should go away after a
week or so. You can apply a vitamin K cream to help them resolve more
quickly. However, the most effective treatment for post injection marks and bruising is the V Beam (pulsed-dye) laser. One treatment can remove the marks in 1 to 2 days.

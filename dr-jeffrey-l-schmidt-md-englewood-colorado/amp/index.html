Statement: Dr. Jeffrey L. Schmidt is a double board-certified facial plastic surgeon practicing in Denver, Colorado. The founder of Schmidt Facial Plastic Surgery, Dr. Schmidt has become a highly sought-out expert in cosmetic and reconstructive enhancements for all areas of the face. He specializes in face and neck lifts, cheek lifts, rhinoplasty, and buccal fat removal, as well as nonsurgical treatments like dermal fillers like Juvéderm and wrinkle relaxers like Botox.

Dr. Schmidt believes that every face tells its own story. For many, that story is marked by life’s challenges, from birth defects to the impact of stress, trauma, or simply aging. Dr. Schmidt’s goal is to show the world the inner beauty of his patients, so their physical appearance more accurately shows how they feel inside.

After graduating from Southern Methodist University, Dr. Schmidt enrolled in medical school at The University of Texas School of Medicine in San Antonio. After obtaining his medical degree, Dr. Schmidt was selected for a head and neck surgery residency in Omaha, Nebraska, where he became Chief Resident during his final year. This was followed by a prestigious facial plastic and reconstructive surgery fellowship under the renowned surgeon Dr. E. Gaylon McCollough, who mentored Dr. Schmidt as he refined his skills in facial cosmetic and reconstructive surgery.

Dr. Schmidt hails from an artistic family, and he initially saw himself as an artist as well—just through a different medium. However, now he realizes the scale of the impact his work has on his patients and their lives. As Dr. Schmidt says, the true payoff for him has less to do with the physical result than the confidence and happiness his patients experience through his work, as well as the “ripple effect” that it has on the other people in their lives.

Dr. Schmidt is board-certified by both the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology in Head and Neck Surgery. He has been voted Top Doctor by Denver’s 5280 Magazine several years in a row.

How Long Should Swelling and Numbness Last After a Facelift?
Swelling after facelift
Jan 20, 2014
It is important to remember to be patient! You just had surgery and you need to give your face time to heal. It can take anywhere from 3 to 4 months for all of the swelling to subside. While you may have already gone back to work, most of the initial swelling has likely gone down. There can still be swelling, and it can be 3 – 4 months before that swelling is completely gone.

Tip Rhinoplasty
A Tip Rhinoplasty
Jan 20, 2014
A “Tip” rhinoplasty is no different from other types of rhinoplasty. This type of rhinoplasty is focused to just treat the tip region of the nose, and not the bridge or other parts of the nose. In general, a tip rhinoplasty helps to:Reduces a bulbous tipReshape a boxy tipAny other modifications that need to be done to improve or refine the tip Typically it is best to do it as an open approach.

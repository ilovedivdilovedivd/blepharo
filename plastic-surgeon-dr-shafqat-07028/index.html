This is a great question. There are several treatment options for a seroma. Aspiration or draining the fluid with a needle and compression is usually the first step. After this, we may recommend a drain placement by a radiologist who can then inject medications into the drain to help the seroma scar down. However, if the seroma has been there for a while, your body likely developed a capsule around it. If this does not work, surgery to remove the fluid and capsule is usually needed.

Tummy tuck

These are great questions!
Tummy tuck candidates are individualized between patients. Usually we recommend being at a BMI of less than 30, but there are patients who are exceptions to that rule. Tissue Expander/Implant Swap. How long do I need to wait?
Time to exchange
Sep 15, 2022
There is no harm in waiting for your exchange surgery. Depending on the type of cancer, there are patients who get radiation to their expanders who then wait 6 months to get the exchange. The biggest risk is rupture of the expander but this is uncommon. It is best to discuss this with your board certified plastic surgeon.

Nipple sensitivity after breast implants
Mar 4, 2020
Hi!
Good question! Short answer is maybe. Large implants, or some access incisions are more likely to cause nerve problems. Removing or replacing your implants with smaller ones may help the problem, but there are no guarantees, as some types of nerve sensitivity are not reversible.

Is there any evidence to indicate that removing textured implants reduces the risk of BIA-
Textured implants and BIA-ALCL
Feb 23, 2020
Hi!
To date there have been no cases of ALCL with smooth implants, so in theory, removing textured implants will remove the risk of ALCL. Having said that, the current recommendation is not to replace textured implants unless you have symptoms.
Make sure to bring up your concerns with your surgeon
All the best!

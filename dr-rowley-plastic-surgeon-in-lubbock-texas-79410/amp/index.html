Want a breast augmentation. Would a lift be necessary also?
Mastopexy
Mar 30, 2022
In my opinion you need a mastopexy (breast lift), this is never scarless, there is no scarless breast lift. A periareolar lift won't be enough and still leaves scars. If you do proceed with augmentation please see a surgeon who has experience with augmenting large breasts and can show you photos of results that would be similar to yours. Good luck, Jane.

I’m 5’7", 178 pounds. Will 475cc be too small?​
Mastopexy with implant
Jul 27, 2021
The problem with doing a lift and implant is that the lift is tightening your skin and the implant will tend to stretch it back out. It's Physics 101-opposing forces. So, 475 is a generous implant. It is difficult to judge without photos, but on most people that will bring you up 2 plus cup sizes. Bigger is not always better, especially when you need a mastopexy. No one is on "Botched" because they are too small, good luck, Jane.

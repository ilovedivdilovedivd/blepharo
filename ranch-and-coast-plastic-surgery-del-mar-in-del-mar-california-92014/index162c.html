Doctors

Paul E. Chasan, MD, FACS

Name: Paul E. Chasan, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Paul E. Chasan has been a board-certified plastic surgeon in Del Mar, California for nearly three decades. The founder of Ranch & Coast Plastic Surgery, Dr. Chasan specializes in facelifts, rhinoplasty, breast augmentation, and revisionary breast surgery.

Using state-of-the-art surgical technologies and techniques, Dr. Chasan also regularly performs tummy tucks (abdominoplasty), liposuction, mommy makeovers, breast lifts, breast reductions, eyelid surgery, and other facial rejuvenation and body contouring procedures.

Dr. Chasan is a former president of the San Diego Plastic Surgery Society. He is considered by many to be a “surgeon’s surgeon,” in that he often operates on other plastic surgeons and their family members.

After earning his medical degree from Creighton University School of Medicine in Omaha, Nebraska, Dr. Chasan went on to complete his residency in General Surgery at the University of California San Diego, where he served as the Chief Resident during his final year. He finished his specialized training during a two-year residency in plastic surgery at the University of Utah in Salt Lake City.

When he is not working at Ranch & Coast Plastic Surgery, Dr. Chasan regularly lectures to plastic surgery residents at the University of California, San Diego. He is also a past board member for Angel Faces, a nonprofit organization that provides education and ongoing support for girls and young women with burn and trauma injuries.

Board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, Dr. Chasan is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS) and a fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS).

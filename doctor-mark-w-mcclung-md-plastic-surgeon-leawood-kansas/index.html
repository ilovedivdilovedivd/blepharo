What to do about drooping eyes?
Eyelid shape
Dec 7, 2020
An in person exam is necessary to determine if you may have slight eyelid ptosis but my impression from four photos is that you do not. You do however have a negative canthal tilt which is a downward slant to the outer corner of the eyelid . The correction for this is a canthoplasty. That said there is nothing abnormal about a small negative canthal tilt

Alternative methods for under eye crease removal besides filler?
tear trough deformity
Sep 3, 2020
You have an underlying anatomic basis for this line that demarcates your eyelid from your cheek. It is a tight insertion of your eyelid muscle to the bone of the orbital rim, referred to as the orbitomalar ligament. It is best treated surgically. In someone young like yourself it can be much improved with fillers and the Hyaluronic acid fillers are a naturally occurring substance in your skin. Please see a board certified plasic surgeon with a good deal of eyelid expertise

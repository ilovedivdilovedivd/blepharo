Doctors

Ali Sajjadian, MD, FACS

Name: Ali Sajjadian, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Ali Sajjadian is a double board-certified plastic surgeon and the founder of Sajjadian Plastic Surgery in Newport Beach, California. He is known not only for his extensive qualifications and surgical skill, but also his compassionate patient care and ability to understand exactly what each patient is looking to achieve. While he excels at numerous surgical procedures, he is most often sought out by patients for his exceptional skill with rhinoplasty and revision rhinoplasty surgeries.

Dr. Sajjadian earned his medical degree from Drexel University College of Medicine in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. His extensive postgraduate career included three residencies: one in general surgery at Temple University School of Medicine in Philadelphia; another in aesthetic, plastic, and reconstructive surgery at the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center; and a third in otolaryngology – head and neck surgery, again at Temple. He then was selected for a prestigious fellowship in facial, plastic, and reconstructive surgery (sponsored by American Academy of Facial Plastic Surgery) at Emory University in Atlanta, Georgia.

Dr. Sajjadian is board certified by the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, the American Board of Plastic Surgery, and the American Board of Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery.

He is also a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, and the Rhinoplasty Society (of which he is a past president), as well as being a fellow of the American College of Surgeons.

Dr. Sajjadian is a former assistant professor of the plastic surgery residency program at the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center.

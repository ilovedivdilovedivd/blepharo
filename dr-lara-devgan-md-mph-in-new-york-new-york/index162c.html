Procedures That Can Make the Face Appear Slimmer
Mar 11, 2019
There are several procedures that can make the face appear slimmer, including buccal fat pad excision, submental liposuction, botox of the masseters, and facial contouring with injectable fillers. Pain, Swelling, Nipple Sensitivity After Breast Augmentation Surgery
Nipple sensitivity after breast augmentation
Jul 26, 2018
Nipple sensitivity and discomfort can occur after breast augmentation surgery due to normal inflammation. However, any concerns you may have should be addressed with your operating surgeon. Icing and gentle compression can assist with pain.
How long is the recovery for breast augmentation? How painful is it? Explain process from the mome
Back to normal 1 month after breast augmentation
Jul 26, 2018
Most patients require narcotic pain medication for 1-3 days after breast augmentation surgery, although use of a long acting nerve block (Exparel) reduces this considerably. Typically patients will take 4-7 days off work, 4-6 weeks off strenuous exercise, and feel "back to normal" about a month after surgery. Implants take 3-6 months to drop and settle into their final position, and scars take a year to mature.
What is the Difference Between Neck Lift and Lower Face Lift?
Necklift vs. Lower Facelift
Jul 10, 2016
There is a continuum between a facelift and necklift, and the differences are neither universally agreed upon nor necessarily clear cut. In my practice, a lower facelift focuses on the jawline, angle of the mandible, and SMAS substructure of the face, while a necklift focuses on the platysma, cervicomental angle, and redraping of neck skin. Commonly, a patient will require a facelift/ necklift to address a combination of these issues.
What Can Be Done to Reduce Swelling and Lumps After Lower Facelift and Platysmaplasty?
How to treat lumps and swelling after necklift
Jul 10, 2016
The best approach is to allow the passage of time. Time alone will allow your scarring to heal, your tissues to mature, your swelling to come down, and your contours to smooth out. In some situations, gentle lymphatic massage can assist. Discuss this with your surgeon.

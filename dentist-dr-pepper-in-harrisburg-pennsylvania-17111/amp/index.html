Bone graft lasting
Nov 9, 2015
It is hard to say where you stand from the photo alone. Bone grafts as well as natural bone tend to resorb over time if not used. Sometimes this can occur rapidly and sometimes very slowly. An x-ray and/or CT scan would be needed to evaluate the amount of bone available at this time to support an implant. IF there is not enough bone present, additional bone grafting can always be performed.

Dental implants for children
Oct 30, 2015
I would strongly advise against dental implants for your child. Implants were not designed for children due to growth issues. Can you get orthognathic surgery without braces?
jaw ssurgery without braces
Oct 29, 2015
If your teeth were aligned with the braces properly to prepare you for surgery, it is possible to perform the surgery without braces. The surgeon will need to attach some arch bars to your teeth to use during the surgery to fixate the teeth in one jaw to the other. The lack of braces will also reduce the ability to perform any fine tuning following the surgery.

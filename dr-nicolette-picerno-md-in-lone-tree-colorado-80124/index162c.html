Is it possible to sweat out ALMI fat transfer injections?
Fat transfer
Jan 19, 2018
Hello, with a fat transfer to the face, the fat that was moved will behave like fat in the location where it was harvested. In general weight changes of 10 pounds or more will have an effect on fat in the face, grafted or not. Also, while weight loss and getting in shape is very good for the body and mind, too much will definitely have an adverse effect on the face. This is more noticeable as we age so if you are under 40 years of age, these changes may be minimal.  Best of luck to you.

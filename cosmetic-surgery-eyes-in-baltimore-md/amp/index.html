Edema or swelling is routine after blepharoplasty and the duration of swelling can depend on position, whether hot temperature cautery was used to cut the skin, other concomitant procedures done, irritation from the suture material, whether orbital fat was removed as well, the degree of tissue disruption during surgery, and other multitude of factors

Blepharoplasty and make-up

Until the incisions are completely healed, I would avoid make-up. Rubbing make-up can open incisions and also can cause an infection.

Give it two weeks at least. If you are concerned about the bruising, then you can probably by cover up to blend the lowest part of the lower lid and cheek, but I would not put any makeup near your incisions

I like to wait for two weeks post op before applying makeup close to the eye lid incision. Mineral makeup is milder and has less effect on the skin in general. Facial makeup away from the incision is okay much sooner

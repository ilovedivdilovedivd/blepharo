Can a platysmaplasty come undone?
Platysmaplasty (neck lift) undone?
Nov 22, 2019
Thank you for asking. The sutures of a platysmaplasty (neck lift or during the neck part of a facelift) could come undone or the muscle itself maybe too thin and wispy to hold sutures. The neck should be carefully evaluated and a course planned according to your anatomy. An in-office procedure is possible on the right neck type. Different plans are needed for different necks and faces. See you local board certified plastic surgeon who can evaluate. Best wishes.

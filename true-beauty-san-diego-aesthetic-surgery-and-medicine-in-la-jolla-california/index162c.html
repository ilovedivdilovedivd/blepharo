Doctors

Brian J. Reagan, MD

Name: Brian J. Reagan, MD
Statement: Dr. Brian J. Reagan is a board-certified plastic surgeon and founder of True Beauty San Diego Aesthetic Surgery and Medicine in La Jolla, California. He also treats patients at CosmetiCare in Newport Beach, California.

An experienced and distinguished surgeon, Dr. Reagan specializes in rhinoplasty surgery. He is also known for his expertise in facelifts and eyelid surgery, as well as breast enhancement, tummy tucks, and mommy makeovers.

His patients also praise his talent as an injector of nonsurgical treatments like Botox and dermal fillers, which he skillfully combines to create a “liquid facelift.”

“I have always dreamed of creating a unique place where the science and art of skin rejuvenation can be delivered in a supervised, private, world-class setting,” says Dr. Reagan. “We use only the most proven products and technology to provide our patients with the best possible results.”

With a long track record of educational excellence, Dr. Reagan trained under recognized leaders in cosmetic and reconstructive surgery, with an emphasis on rhinoplasty.

Dr. Reagan earned his medical degree from Weill Cornell Medicine (formerly Cornell University Medical College) in New York City. He went on to complete multiple residencies, including one in General Surgery at New York-Presbyterian/Weill Cornell Medical Center (formerly The New York Hospital at Cornell Medical Center); another in General Surgery at Harbor-UCLA Medical Center in Los Angeles; and a third in Plastic Surgery at The University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center at Dallas. He was then selected for prestigious fellowships at New York-Presbyterian/Weill Cornell Medical Center Burn Center and The Wound Healing Laboratory.

Dr. Reagan is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He is a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, the California Society of Plastic Surgeons, and the San Diego Plastic Surgery Society.

He is also the recipient of numerous awards, including being named one of the Best Plastic Surgeons in San Diego by San Diego Magazine and one of the Best Plastic Surgeons in La Jolla by La Jolla Light Newspaper.

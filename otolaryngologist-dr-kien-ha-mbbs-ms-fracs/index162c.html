Is it possible to stop my nose drooping when I smile?
Droopy nose rhinoplasty
Jul 18, 2019
This is a very common question with many rhinoplasty patients. There are a number of maneuvers that can be performed as part of the rhinoplasty operation that will help stabilise the tip and prevent it drooping when you smile. One established technique is called an open structured rhinoplasty utilizing a septal extension graft and tip suspension. Find an experienced facial plastic surgeon specializing in rhinoplasty to discuss with. Best wishes

Will Rhinoplasty still go ahead with eye infection?
Conjunctivitis prior to rhinoplasty surgery
Jul 18, 2019
Generally speaking, it shouldn’t be an issue at all. Presuming that your local doctor has already started topical eye drops, a minor conjunctival infection should settle within a few days. Even if it hasn’t completely, it shouldn’t affect the ability to perform a rhinoplasty or the outcome. Best inform your rhinoplasty surgeon as soon as possible and get their personal opinion. Best wishes.

Dent on the side of my nose. How can I fix it?
Asymmetrical Tip
Jul 18, 2019
The dent on the left side of your nose is a result of the underlying tip cartilage shape (Lower lateral cartilage). If you do not have a history of previous nasal surgery or significant trauma, it is most likely congenital in nature. The best way to address this would be with an open structured rhinoplasty performed by an experienced Facial Plastic Surgeon or Plastic surgeon.

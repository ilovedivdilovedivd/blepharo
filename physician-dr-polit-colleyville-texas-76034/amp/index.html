Does Botox an effective treatment for under arm sweat?
Botox (and other neurotoxins) for treatment of excessive underarm sweating
Feb 3, 2021
All of the neurotoxins, Botox, Dysport, and Xeomin, are great for treating excessive sweating under the arms. It is important though that a high enough quantity is used per armpit. For severe cases, retreatment can be needed every 6 months. Over time, my patients have been able to space out their treatments to once a year and some have even had the problem resolve completely after a few years of repeated treatments.

Under eye wrinkles when smiling, any treatment suggestions to soften them?
Under eye wrinkles when smiling
Feb 2, 2021
Wrinkles indicate volume loss whether it’s due to aging, weight loss, sun damage, or other. Smile lines are a combination of the volume loss along with the mechanical forces of the muscles pushing up the cheeks to show a “smile”. There are several options for treating under eye wrinkles when smiling. Careful injection with fine fillers, PRP, or even your own nanofat can provide structural support to decrease the appearance of these smile lines.

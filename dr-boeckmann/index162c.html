How long should I wait between Dysport treatments?
Aug 31, 2023
Most patients retreat within 3-4 months from their treatment in order to maintain the benefits. Keeping a consistent treatment schedule tends to improve the longevity of the result by keeping a baseline amount of Dysport at work in the muscle. Patients also notice a faster onset of the Dysport when they keep their treatments scheduled at regular intervals.
Flying after lip filler?
Flying after lip filler
Aug 11, 2023
Yes, It is ok to fly after getting lip filler. I always recommend cool compress to the lip after the treatment to minimize swelling/bruising.
Tonsillectomy after lip filler?
Lip Filler before Tonsillectomy
Aug 11, 2023
The most conservative approach would be waiting until you are healed from your tonsillectomy prior to getting lip filler. During a tonsillectomy a mouth guard is placed around the teeth and lips which may put a small amount of stretch on the lip. This could result in a small risk of interfering with the lip filler because dermal fillers settle into place for the first 3 weeks after placement.

Will a neck lift fix my dimpled chin?
Improving chin contour
Aug 10, 2023
The neck lift will not improve the contour of the chin. Chin dimpling and bumpiness can occur due to excessive contraction of the underlying chin muscle (mentalis muscle). Botox can be used in this area to soften the muscle and improve the overlying skin contour. Any contour issues which persist can then be carefully treated with dermal fillers.

Statement: Dr. Mehmet Atila is a specialist in Plastic and Aesthetic Surgery and has an additional certification in Hand Surgery. He is a member of the European Board of Plastic Reconstructive and Aesthetic Surgery (EBOPRAS).
He started his residency in the Department of Surgery at the Osnabrück Municipal Hospital, afterwards he continued his training in the Department of Plastic and Aesthetic Surgery at the Agaplesion Diakonieklinikum Rotenburg.

At the Medical Inn Zentrum, the patient’s wishes and comfort come first. In order to achieve excellent results, the best medical expertise and innovative technologies are used. The goal of the Medical Inn Zentrum is to provide the highest quality of services at all times. Safety, individual approach and patient satisfaction are guaranteed in order to achieve this goal, using all possible means.

The clinic’s offer not only includes liposuction, but also male surgeries, facial plastic surgery procedures, breast augmentation surgeries, lipedema treatments and hair transplant. What is more, there are also non-invasive procedures available, such as Botox injections and laser skin rejuvenation.

The clinic also provides scars or skin lesions removal. Vaginal tightening, buttock augmentation without surgery and the insertion of a gastric balloon are also part of the wide range of offered treatments. Last but not least, Medical Inn Zentrum offers a so-called Mommy Makeover which combines several operations and procedures in one which includes, among others, tummy tuck and body lifting.

Medical Inn has several locations in different German cities. All clinics are equipped according to the highest standards and are located in central areas of the city. The facilities also satisfy particularly high demands in terms of comfort and aesthetics. All the individual facilities are linked to each other. In addition, Medical Inn has three large surgical centers.

To fulfill your desires without compromising, Medical Inn offers financing starting at 0%. You can conveniently set up an installment plan for a 12-month period. The clinic will take care of follow-up insurance for you, because your safety is our top priority. If German is not your native language, the clinic’s interpreting services are available for you.

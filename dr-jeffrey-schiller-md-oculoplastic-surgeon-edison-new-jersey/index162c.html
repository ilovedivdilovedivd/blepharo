Will blepharoplasty open my eyes up?
Blepharoplasty with lifting of the sub-brow fat pad will help
Dec 2, 2018
An upper blepharoplasty will sculpt the fullness and improve the crease and flatten the space above your eyelashes. Imbrication of the orbicularis sometimes called a “brassiere” suture will lift the sub-brow fat out of your eyelids without a browlift. Upper blepharoplasty is generally done with local anesthesia or local and sedation. General anesthesia is not necessary.

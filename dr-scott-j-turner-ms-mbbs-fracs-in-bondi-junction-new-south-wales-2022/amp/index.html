Burning sensation in one breast 8 months after breast augmentation, ruptured ??
Nerve Related Pain
Mar 9, 2019
Hi
Burning sensation in the breast after breast augmentation is very common. Often the nerves supplying the breast are stretched / bruised and when they wake up / start healing you often get a nerve type of pain which can be stabbing / sharp pain or burning type of sensation which often settles over the first 3 to 6 months.
It would be very unlikely to be an implant rupture at this early stage but best for you to follow up with your surgeon and discuss your concerns

With tuberous breast we often add fat grafting to the lower pole of the breast to help round out the breast shape and small periareolar lift to elevate the areolar
Proper way to treat breast augmentation infection?
Breast Implant Infections
Feb 8, 2019
Hi
Breast implant infections can be very serious if not treated correctly.
Most often this might be a minor wound issue at the incision line and will settle with simple measures, but if there is a true infection around the implant this may need other treatment, including removal of the implant for 3-6 months.
If you still have fluid leaking out after 2 months than a second opinion may be warranted and also an ultrasound to see if there is fluid surrounding the implant.

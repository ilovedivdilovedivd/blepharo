Does lower eyelid surgery reduce the surface area of the dark circles?
Yes it will
Aug 1, 2023
Yes, with skin pinch, some of the excess darkened skin will be removed. The incision will be hidden under the lashes. Please visit with a board-certified plastic surgeon. Good luck!
Blepharoplasty combination approach for under eye bags?
Transcutaneous lower blepharoplasty with removal of fat and skin
Aug 1, 2023
Your best option is a transcutaneous lower blepharoplasty with removal of fat and skin. You could benefit from laser resurfacing in addition to this, but this will not remove the need for surgery. Please visit with a board-certified plastic surgeon. Good luck!
Can a Blepharoplasty help hooded eyes and uneven folds?
Upper blepharoplasty
Aug 1, 2023
Hi yes an upper blepharoplasty will remove some of the extra skin and make your eyelid creases more symmetric. You are a good candidate for the procedure. Please visit with a board-certified plastic surgeon. Good luck!
Should I go with partial or full incision?
Full incision
Aug 1, 2023
You have excess skin and asymmetry which is best corrected with the full incision approach. It allows better visualization and has less chance of recurrence/failure. Please visit with a board-certified plastic surgeon. Good luck!
Double eyelid surgery in 2021, is it possible to (re)establish symmetry?
Yes it is possible to improve your asymmetry!
Aug 1, 2023
It is definitely possible to improve your asymmetry with revision surgery. This can be performed under local anesthesia. Your understanding of the procedure is exactly correct. You will need one crease to be re-established as it has failed on that side. The other side will need slight correction with lowering of the crease (it is too high) and removing just a small amount of skin. Please visit with a board-certified plastic surgeon. Good luck!

Is it safe to use retinol or vitamin C serum after Derma Rolling?
Non-Surgical treatments
Feb 26, 2019
Thank you for your question. Usually because these products are acidic if you apply them after a derma roller treatment they will give you a burning sensation and the retinol can burn your skin easier. I would wait 12-24 hours before applying these products.
Best of luck,
Dr.Kolenda
Would Derma Rolling improve fine lines and acne scars?
Non-Surgical treatments
Feb 26, 2019
Thank you for your question. Microneedling is a great way of making your body produce collagen which in turn will improve fine lines and acne scars. Typically for 'at home' treatments the dermaroller is only 1-2 mm deep and is not very painful. Some dermarollers that are used in a medical clinic setting with topical anesthesia can be deeper for a more intense treatment.
Best of luck,
Dr.Kolenda
Do botox in the masseter muscle stay permanent?
Neurotoxin Treatments
Feb 26, 2019
Thank you for your question. If you maintain the treatments then the effects that you get from your masseter treatments will be upheld but once you stop the treatments, your muscles will strengthen again and you will go back to a sore jaw & clenching.
Best of luck,
Dr.Kolenda
Botox, fillers or microneedling?
Non-Surgical treatments
Feb 26, 2019
Thank you for your questions. I would suggest that if you want to improve and do a pro-active treatment that you look in to microneedling. This treatment will induce collegen production and preserve elasticity. I would not suggest adding any volume or treating lines with botox to your face as you are young and don't need these treatments at this time.
Best of luck,
Dr.Kolenda

Feb 26, 2019
Thank you for your question. It sounds like there were too many units injected but you should clarify with the injector what areas were treated. My lips are uneven after masseter Botox. What options do I have?
Neurotoxin Treatments
Feb 26, 2019
Thank you for your questions. Unfortunately after your masseters were treated with the neurotoxin, a muscle that controls your smile was effected. This is something that you need to wait for to go away on its' own. This issue can resolve anywhere between 2 weeks- 3 months.
Best of luck,
Dr.Kolenda
Should I do Botox or wait ?

Feb 26, 2019
Hello. Thank you for your question. Based off your photo and what your concerns are, I would say that you are a great candidate for botox in your forehead.

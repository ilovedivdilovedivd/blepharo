How long do I need to wait if I had Fraxel treatment a week ago before considering Morpheus?
Wait 4 weeks, Be Patient with your skin
Sep 4, 2019
In general we advise to wait a minimum of 4 weeks, sometimes longer, in between any energy, or chemically based aesthetic treatments. This includes injectables, microneedling, laser, IPL, RadioFrequency energy, and chemical peels. This is to allow healing and recovery from each treatment, avoiding risks such as infections or post-inflammatory pigment changes.

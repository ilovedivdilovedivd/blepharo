Statement: Dr. Jamil Asaria, MD, is a double board-certified facial plastic surgeon based in Toronto, Canada. He specializes in rhinoplasty, facelifts, neck lifts and hair transplants. Dr. Asaria is known for consistently delivering balanced and natural results, with compassion, understanding, and the utmost care.

At his practice, FACE Toronto, Dr. Asaria also performs a wide range of noninvasive aesthetic procedures. These include hair restoration, nonsurgical facelifts, and a variety of injectables, like Juvéderm, Botox, Voluma, Radiesse, and Restylane, among others.

Dr. Asaria’s educational background and academic credentials are highly impressive. After obtaining his medical degree and completing a subsequent otolaryngology residency at the University of Toronto, he fellowship trained at the University of Michigan under internationally renowned surgeon Dr. Shan Baker. Dr. Asaria’s many achievements range from placing first out of all surgeons in North America on the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery certifying examination, to having one of the highest scores in the history of the exam.

Among his many honors and accolades, he is the recipient of both the Jack R. Anderson Prize for Scholastic Excellence and the Canadian Network for International Surgery Award. He is board-certified by both the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada for Head & Neck Surgery.

With his unparalleled surgical talents and extensive expertise, Dr. Asaria has become a lauded expert in his field. Currently, he is the Canadian Region Director for the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery. He also serves as the clinical instructor and chief examiner for the division of facial plastic and reconstructive surgery at the University of Toronto. Dr. Asaria is also on the advisory boards for Allergan, Merz, and Galderma.

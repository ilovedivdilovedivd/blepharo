Doctors

Shahin Fazilat, MD, FACS

Name: Shahin Fazilat, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Shahin Fazilat is a board-certified cosmetic and reconstructive plastic surgeon and founder of SF Bay Area Plastic Surgery, with offices in Mountain View, Los Gatos and Morgan Hill, California. He has expertise in a wide variety of procedures, but Dr. Fazilat specializes in breast enhancement, tummy tucks, and complete mommy makeovers, as well as facial procedures like rhinoplasty, eyelid lifts, and lip lifts. He also offers popular nonsurgical facial rejuvenation treatments like Botox, dermal fillers, laser resurfacing, Microneedling, and vaginal rejuvenation, as well as body contouring treatments like BodyTite, Facetite, Morpheus8, CoolSculpting, and CoolTone.

Dr. Fazilat received his Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Maryland and went on to earn his medical degree at Howard University School of Medicine. He completed two residencies, one in general surgery at The Washington Hospital Center, and another in plastic surgery at Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. He pursued a cosmetic fellowship at Virginia Hospital Center in Arlington, Virginia and a plastic surgery fellowship at Stanford University, focused on micro/reconstructive surgery and hand surgery.

Over the course of his education, Dr. Fazilat was consistently invited to participate in competitive research programs, including The National Institutes of Health (NIH) Fellowship Program. This research has not only informed his skillset as an esteemed plastic surgeon but has allowed him to serve as an authority in the field. To date, he has authored and co-authored over a dozen publications.

In addition to being board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, Dr. Fazilat is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS) and the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ASAPS). He is currently an adjunct faculty/clinical instructor at Stanford University in the Division of Plastic Surgery.

When he is not treating his patients or training future plastic surgeons, Dr. Fazilat works with breast cancer patients at the El Camino Hospital Cancer Center. He also works overseas with SmileTrain (and similar organizations) helping to improve the quality of life for people with cleft lips and palates.

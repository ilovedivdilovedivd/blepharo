Rhinoplasty recovery

Pain is minimal. The nasal cast, when present, comes off at the first post-operative appointment, one week after surgery. Do I need a breast lift or is an augmentation with an implant sufficient?
Breast augmentation with or without a lift
Dec 29, 2020
A breast augmentation alone can definitely help lift the nipple. However, the amount of lift may be inadequate and a breast lift may be necessary in order to reposition the nipple at the center of the breast mount. In your case the amount of ptosis (droopiness) appears to be minimal. I would recommend a breast augmentation with a peri-areolar lift. This procedure allows small amount of lift, the incision is just around the areola and would optimize the breast augmentation result.
Am I suitable for a lift without implants?
Breast lift without implants
Dec 29, 2020
A breast lift alone would reduce the size of your areola and give you an improved breast shape. However, the only downside of not having an implant is possibly the lack of fullness at the upper aspect of the breast (upper breast pole). Many women are ok with a more natural tear drop breast that is perky and are happy with just a lift. I hope this helps.

Skin changes after facelift
Dec 28, 2020
It is normal to experience changes in your skin as part of the healing process.

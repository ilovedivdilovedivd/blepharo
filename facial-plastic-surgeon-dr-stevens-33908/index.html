Answer Snippet
Type of surgeon for facelift?
Research
Jun 21, 2021
Consulting with multiple plastic surgeons and facial plastic surgeons is highly recommended. Get a feel of who you feel comfortable with and confident about. The benefits of consulting with a facial plastic surgeon is that they are highly specialized in the face and in general have a lot of experience with facial plastic surgery.
Can Botox help these eye wrinkles?
Botox and possible pinch blepharoplasty
Jun 21, 2021
Botox is great for the wrinkles that occur with motion which would target the crows feet or "smile lines". In regards to the wrinkles directly under the eyes, it may be worth it to ask your plastic surgeon about pinch blepharoplsty, which removes the extra skin under the eyes.
Does botox make it harder to pull the upper lip downwards?
It can
Jun 21, 2021
If too much botox is injected into this area, it can affect certain motions. Such as, drinking out of a straw which in a sense is the motion to "pull the lips together".
Is Morpheus 8 safe? I am 43 years old with rheumatoid arthritis.
Yes
Jun 21, 2021
Treating with Morpheus8 should not affect your arthritis. Following up with your rheumatologist may provide you with any additional information.
Why did my lip Volbella only last 4 months?
This is average lifespan
Jun 15, 2021
Volbella is a very fine/smooth filler used for "smokers lines". The reason this filler does not last very long is because it is around an area of motion. This is pretty standard for this type of filler in this area.
Buccal Fat Removal "reversal" procedure?
Bellafill
Jun 15, 2021
We commonly use Bellafill to fill in this area as well as other areas. Bellafill is a five year filler that fills in volume loss which can in a sense "fix" the problem.
Would a chin implant help improve my profile and appearance?
Yes
Jun 15, 2021
A chin implant would be a great way to enhance the chin. This would provide you with more projection and a smooth transition from the chin to the jawline. Chin implants are commonly done with Rhinoplasty surgery.
Am I a candidate for submental lipo or is my jaw not strong enough?
Evoke
Jun 15, 2021
Evoke facial contouring may be a good option for you. It uses radiofrequency energy (heat) to break down fat and tighten the skin of the submental region. This is a great way to avoid surgery.
How do people with dry eyes have a facelift?
Eye drops
Jun 15, 2021
Sometimes during surgery, the eyes are left open. It is important to tell your surgeon this so that they leave the eyes closed as well as use lubricating eyedrops during surgery.
Time for a lower face and neck lift (42)?
Can be avoided
Jun 15, 2021
Face and neck lift can be avoided with collagen stimulating treatments such as, Morpheus8 radiofrequency microneedling. Morpheus8 stimulates collagen, can tighten the skin and improve fine lines and wrinkles. This can give you a few years to avoid a facelift.
What can I do for a more " open " look?
Endoscopic browplasty
Mar 12, 2021
Endoscopic browplasty would be something that you could benefit from. This is done by making small incisions in the scalp and using devices known as Endotines. This elevates the brow into a higher position providing you with a more awake look.
How many units of Botox will this need?
20
Mar 12, 2021
At our office we use the standard dose of 20 units in the glabella. This looks like it is a very strong muscle for you. If you notice movement with 20 units, you can always get additional units after a week.
Is there a possibility of hitting a nerve during FaceTite for neck?
Yes
Mar 12, 2021
There is a chance of nerve damage with this procedure. However, Facetite is done safely by using a large amount of tumescent which provides a safety feature by "puffing" up the area to stay away from the nerves. In regards to the two vertical bands, these are called platysma bands, which are muscles. These muscles cannot be treated with Facetite. These muscles would either be improved with surgery or with botox. There is a fair amount of swelling, mostly due to the amount of tumescent used.
Will a lip lift change my nose?
No
Mar 10, 2021
Although the suturing is done around the nose, A surgical lip lift should not affect the shape of your nose.
Do I need temple filler for a smoother, more rounded look?
Yes
Mar 10, 2021
Hollow temples are commonly treated with filler. We love to use Bellafill at our practice. It can act as a long lasting "facial implant" providing you will a softer, rounder look.

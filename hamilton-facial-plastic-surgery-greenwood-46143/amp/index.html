Is It possible to smooth the entire forehead with Botox, but keep natural movement of the brows?
Lines above the brow and raising of eyebrow
Nov 4, 2019
Unfortunately, you can not have a raised brow and smooth forehead if you do have lines just above the brow.   Injecting right above the brow in most cases will cause the eyebrow to droop.  It is recommended that all injections are at least 1 centimeter above the brow to prevent drooping.  In some cases, adding  Botox to the tail of the brow can  help minimize these lines.  Seeing an experienced injector or facial plastic surgeon for your Botox treatment would be ideal.

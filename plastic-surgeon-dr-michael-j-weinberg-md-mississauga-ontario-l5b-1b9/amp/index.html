Will fillers help to remove my lip scarring?
Using fillers to address lip scarring
Oct 13, 2022
Based on the photograph, it would appear you have a single scar on the lower lip that is raised, but not discolored. It is possible injecting filler on either side of the raised scar might help conceal it. However, this is not always successful. There are other treatments that could be considered for raised scars-I would advise seeing a board certified dermatologist or plastic surgeon for an in person appointment so they can examine your lip and discuss options.
Reducing/smoothing out the cupid’s bow – possible?
Possible to smooth Cupid’s bow
Oct 13, 2022
Yes, it is possible to reduce the prominence of the cupids bow when performing lip injections. However, it is not usually possible to eliminate it all together. 
Do my lips even qualify for the small amount of Lip Filler?
Proportion is key
Oct 13, 2022
Whenever Lip injections are undertaken, the goal should be to achieve good proportion between the upper and lower lip. You do have quite a small upper lip relative to your lower lip. A small amount of filler in your upper lip might improve the proportion without changing the overall shape.
It’s been 8 months since my last syringe – Why is there a white border around my lip?
Filler too close to the surface
Oct 13, 2022
The commonest reason for a white appearance after filler injections is placement of the product too superficially ( ie too close to the skin’s surface), resulting in what is called a tyndall effect that is caused by the way light is reflected by the product. It can often take several repeat injections with hyaluronidase to get rid of the unwanted filler, which can be quite stubborn when it is located in the superficial layers of the skin.

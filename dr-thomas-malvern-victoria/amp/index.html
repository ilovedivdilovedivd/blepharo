Dark pockets under eyes which can not be explained. Thoughts or advice?
Lower Bleph
Jun 7, 2019
You appear to have prominent fat pads on the lower eyelids with herniation or laxity of the orbital septum. This creates a shadow effect resulting in dark lines.
I would recommend a fat reduction surgical technique such as transconjunctival partial fat pad excision and re draping in to the tear trough area with some spare fat.
Non surgical option would be the use of filler.
Some patients have natural skin pigmentation in addition to any shadow effect, this is difficult to treat.

Can fat transfer be conducted at the same time as removing implants?
Lipofilling breast after implant removal
May 24, 2019
Hi, that’s a great question. Implant removal at the same time as fat injection is becoming one of my more common procedures. Removing implants with complete capsulectomy takes away any foreign material and lipofilling the breast during the same operation helps to provide some volume and shape which would not be present with implant removal alone. Only a modest amount of fat can be injected at one go so any volume increase is only ever 100 – 150cc per breast. Hence it may need to be repeated…

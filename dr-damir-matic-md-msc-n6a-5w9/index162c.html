What is the recurrence of dehiscence in the axillary region after revision? 1 week post-op.
Dehiscence after brachioplasty
Jun 26, 2017
Dehiscence after brachioplasty can occur in 10-20% of patients depending on how much was removed and overall size as well as other issues like if you are a smoker, diabetic, or even post-bariatric. It also most commonly will occur up near the armpit but of course can happen anywhere along the incision.

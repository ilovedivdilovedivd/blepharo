Tummy tuck and venous insufficiency?
Tummy tuck and venous insufficiency
Feb 14, 2023
Hello and thank you for your question. I don't think that the varicose veins are a particular contraindication for a tummy tuck, but a tummy tuck will cause slowing of venous return to your heart from your legs. This may cause a blood clot in your veins that can go to your heart and lungs. For that reason I put all my patients on blood thinner (Lovenox) after the surgery. Consult with your surgeon for more information on the subject.

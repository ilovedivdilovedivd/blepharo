Nose still swollen from rhinoplasty after a year. Should I get it corrected?
post op swelling
Apr 22, 2015
It depends on your skin type however swelling can take years
to settle down, and the longer you wait before considering revision the better.
We have had patients come in who are 4 years post op and their nose looks even
better and more refined than it did at 2 years post op!
6 months post op Rhinoplasty, how risky is a revision?
Revision rhino waiting
Apr 22, 2015
It is best to wait at least 12-18months before considering
revision surgery. This allows your nose to fully heal and settle down and
allows for the swelling to subside which would show the true results of your
surgery and what could potentially need to be done to correct it.

Apr 22, 2015
It would be best to wait until you are about 18 to consider
rhinoplasty to ensure you are fully grown. Rhinoplasty and alar reduction
surgery can help change the shape of your nostrils.

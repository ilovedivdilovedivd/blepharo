Wait Until After Children for Breast Augmentation?
Children and Breast Augmentation
May 14, 2019
A breast augmentation timing question is one that is a very iportant decision. I generally advise patient’s who are going to have children in the near future to wait until after pregnancy to have the procedure. The reason being is that breast feeding and the enlargement of the breast may change the shape and size of the breast. Patients can certainly breast feed after a breast augmentation.

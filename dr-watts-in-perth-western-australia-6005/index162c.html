Will Removal of chin implant cause sagging, deformed chin?
Will Removal of chin implant cause sagging, deformed chin?
Jan 10, 2019
Thanks for the question and images. It is true that if the skin and soft tissues are stretched over an implant then removal may lead to some sagging. It is impossible to predict 100% but the solution is a skin tightening procedure whether that be a non surgical or surgical technique. You can have the implant modified or the surgeon could fixate the wings of the implants with screws to improve the contour. Good luck.RegardsDr Guy Watts

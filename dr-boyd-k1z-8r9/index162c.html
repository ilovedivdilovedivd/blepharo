Yeast Infection Before Breast Augmentation
Jun 16, 2017
I’m sorry that happened to you!  If you were my patient, I would definitely recommend waiting until the yeast infection has been treated and resolved.  An implant is a foreign body and the single biggest problem can be infection around the implant.  We want the pocket that the implant is going to be placed into to be as sterile as possible.  Ideally an elective procedure can be deferred until the patient is healthy and fit to minimize the risk of complications.  Good luck!

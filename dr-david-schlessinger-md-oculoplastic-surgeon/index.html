Can my asymmetry from eyelid surgery 2 years ago be fixed?
Eye Asymmetry
Apr 7, 2020
It appears that your left upper eyelid (right side on the photo) is slightly lower and you are compensating by lifting your eyebrow. You likely need a revision of the ptosis repair on that side. Ptosis repairs have a high rate of revision due to many factors including neural input from the brain and due to the the muscle moving during the healing period. I recommend you seek the opinion of an oculoplastic surgeon that does a lot of ptosis repairs.
What type of eye surgery is most beneficial for me to get rid of sleepy eyes? photos)
Drooping Eyelids
Apr 7, 2020
The "sleepy" appearance of your eyelids is due to ptosis, or drooping of the eyelids. This can be repaired by a surgical procedure that tightens the lifting muscles of the eyelids. This is a routine operation and can be performed under local anesthesia. I would recommend seeking the opinion of an Oculoplastic Surgeon. In my office we can demonstrate what the result would be by placing eyedrops that temporarily lifts the muscle.'

Will tear trough or cheek filler help these dark circles?
Under Eyelid Filler
Dec 3, 2019
It is difficult to tell excactly what is happening from your pictures.  The junction between the cheek and lower eyelid (tear trough area) is a common place to have loss of volume which can result in dark circles and an aged appearance. If cheek filler is administered without addressing the tear trough the appearance of volume loss can be worsened. You should speak to your Surgeon or seek the opinion of a Surgeon skilled in injecting tear troughs.

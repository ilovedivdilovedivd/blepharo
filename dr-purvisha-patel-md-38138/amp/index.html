It is typical for patients to require 6 vials for full facial treatment, but not in one sitting, as the filler should be added over a period of time. I usually inject 2 vials, and then add as needed 6-8 weeks later. Without seeing a picture however, it is hard to tell if you would really need 6 vials on your first sitting...
I have continued numbness 2 weeks after injections. Is this common?
numbness after Kybella injections
Nov 7, 2015
Pain, swelling and numbness are all to be expected after Kybella injections as the area experiences a strong inflammatory reaction to the injection/fat cells dissolving. This inflammation and swelling, can push down on the nerves in the area, and as the inflammation subsides, the feeling will come back.

My bruising seems to be getting worse after 3 days. Should I be concerned?
Bruising of lips after Restylane
May 5, 2015
Bruising after filler injection to lips is common. It can appear to get worse if the patient is on blood thinners/takes asprin/ takes vitamin E/ drinks red wine/ takes ginkgo. Ice immediately after injection helps. Taking suppliments containing arnika help as well. If it continues to worsen, consult your injecting physician

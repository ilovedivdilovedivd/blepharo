What are the risks of sliding genioplasty?
Genioplasty risks.
Jul 31, 2018
It is possible to have weakness of the lower lip depressors if the marginal mandibular nerve is injured. This is very rare. Stretching of the mental nerve, which provides sensation to the lower lip is much more frequent particularly for the first 6 weeks or so but almost always settles with time.

Weak chin and jaw, chin implant or genioplasty? 24 yo and unsatisfied with my chin (se
Genioplasty vs Chin Implant
Jul 31, 2018
Hi
from your photos you do appear to be “retrogenic” which means your chin projection is reduced. This appearance may be improved with either a genioplasty or a chin implant. In my opinion a genioplasty will give you a more natural result which will be more stable in the long run. The procedure is done via a small incision inside the lower lip and has a very small risk of complications. I would suggest you meet with an experienced craniomaxillofacial surgeon to discuss your options.

What is the best choice of lip augmentations for cleft lips?
Fat or Filler
Jul 30, 2018
Hi
fillers can be used to augment but the result won’t be permanent. Fat grafting, either as a fat injection or a “dermis fat graft” can give a longer lasting result but it is not always successful. Sometimes “top ups” are required to achieve the result you are looking for. I would suggest that you discuss this with your cleft lip and palate surgeon.
Good luck
David

Results are generally not as good as when the surgery is performed in infancy but often good improvements can be achieved.

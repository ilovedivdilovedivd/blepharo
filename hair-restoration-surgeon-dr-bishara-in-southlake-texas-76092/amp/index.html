Can scar revision with artas or neograft conceal a hair transplant donor strip scar?
scar revision
Jan 5, 2018
Most young male patients can scar well. Too well sometimes bc the body’s ability to lay down scar is robust at an early age. If the scar has a blood supply it can be transplanted. WE simple poke it with a needle and if it bleeds it will succeed. You must make sure the angle is acute enough to help hide the scar- common problem. Sometimes the best results come from scalp micropigmention especially if you wish to shave the head or wear the hair short…

ARTAS Technology: How does it actually work in easy-to-understand terms?
How the ARTAS works
Jan 5, 2018
The ARTAS works by using a robotic arm and comprised interface to dissect out follicular units for transplanting by hand. It can also make recipient sites to place the grafts into. It does not place the grafts yet.

Dr Mark A Bishara, MD, FISHRS
Paragon Hair Clinics, DFW, Texas

For smaller cases in experienced hands both will provide excellent results.

ARTAS – am I a candidate?
Patchy hair loss vs patterned hair loss
Jan 5, 2018
The ARTAS is FDA cleared for men. While women were not studied in the 510K we have successfully transplanted women using this device. More importantly, you will need a complete work up for the cause of your hair loss fist to determine if the ARTAS is the best device or if transplant would predictably be good option for you.

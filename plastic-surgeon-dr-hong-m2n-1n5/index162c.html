Can you get a tattoo after a breast augmentation?
Can You Get A Tattoo After A Breast Augmentation?
Dec 6, 2017
Sure you can get a tattoo after a breast augmentation but you just have to ensure that your surgeon has confirmed that you are fully healed. Also it is important that you make sure all the tattoo equipment are sterile.  
Can Breast Augmentation Cause the Nipples to Flatten?
Can Breast Augmentation Cause The Nipples To Flatten?
Dec 6, 2017
Yes it can. In breast augmentation, nipples get flatten as a result of the type of incisions or probably due to the fact that they are stretched out with the placement of the breast implants but with time, the nipples will eventually turn out okay, it is only a matter of time and shouldn’t be a serious concern.
Is It Okay to Take Weight Loss Supplements After a Month from a Breast Augmentation?
Is It Okay To Take Weight Loss Supplements After A Month From A Breast Augmentation?
Dec 6, 2017
You may take supplements one month after your breast augmentation and you have recovered uneventfully after your surgery. Please consult with your doctor to make sure the type of supplements have no harmful effect to your health.
Can Breast Muscles Be Tightened to Create More Cleavage?
Can Breast Muscle Be Tightened To Create More Cleavage?
Dec 6, 2017
It your breasts are too separated you can discuss your concern with your surgeon. It is possible that you may need more release of your pocket medially. Also placing your implants in a different pocket may also be helpful. Please discuss your concerns with your surgeon.
Is it ok to go without a bra 3 weeks post BA?
Is It Ok To Go Without A Bra 3 Weeks Post Breast Augmentation?
Dec 6, 2017
Every surgeon has his or her routine with post operative bra use. I usually allow patients to go without their bras for 2-3 weeks as long as it is convenient for them.
Is it normal to have burning sensation after stitches splitting?
Is It Normal To Have Burning Sensation After Stitches Splitting?
Dec 6, 2017
Yes it is normal to have burning sensation after stitches split. Usually if you keep your wound clean the split should stop within a few days and the burning sensation should improve.
Is There A High Chance Of Implants Bursting or Causing Other Problems?
Is There A High Chance Of Implants Bursting Or Causing Other Problems?
Dec 6, 2017
As a surgeon, I must make you understand that implants are not made to last forever. The manufacturers are different and they all have their lifespan. In general implants can last at least 10 years and many patients have their implants for over 25 to 30 years with no signs of rupture.

Removal and replacement of smaller implants with mastopexy?
minor procedure after
Oct 27, 2023
If you’re having smaller implants placed the surgeon may be able to excise all of the areola along the scar line. If your surgeon isn’t able to excise all areola along the scar line during your surgery, this could probably be fixed later as a minor procedure several months after your lift once the tissues have healed and relaxed a bit.
Do people who have lipo of lower neck have good results usually?
lower neck liposuction
Oct 27, 2023
Liposuction of the upper neck under the chin (near the jaw) usually has better results than the lower neck (closer to your chest). If the lower neck needs liposuction, this usually needs to be done at the same time as a necklift to lift up and tighten the skin and remove the extra skin left behind.
All areas that get liposuction need compression after to minimize swelling and help the skin “stick” down.

Can permanent sutures for symmastia repair be removed?
maybe
Oct 27, 2023
If implants are coming out your surgeon may be able to remove the sutures if they’re easy to find. If the sutures aren’t causing a problem, hunting for them when they’re hard to find or see can cause more problems and inflammation and disrupt skin adherence to the chest wall.

Will non dissolvable sutures come out if a piece of it is accidentally left?
Epicanthoplasty sutures
Sep 28, 2017
Hi there
If the sutures have been removed there should be no issues. If there is a stitch that is running between the inside of your eyelid and the outside you can either get it trimmed flush with the skin and allow the skin to heal over or you may have to see someone to have them remove it safely for you!
This can be done under local Anaesthtic and should not hurt at all. If in doubt go see your doctor. Hope that helps!

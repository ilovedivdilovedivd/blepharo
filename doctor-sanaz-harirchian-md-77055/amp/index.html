How much can the shape of your nose be altered in rhinoplasty?
Lengthening the nose
Mar 10, 2019
Hello. Yes it is possible to counterrotate the nasal tip or "bring it down." This involves lengthening the nasal tip. This can be done with several different techniques depending on the rest of the nose. Good luck. 
I just was curious how my nose ended up this way?
Rhinoplasty questions
Mar 10, 2019
Hi. Your concerns are understandable. You have a combination of a retracted ala + a hanging columella. Both can be improved & addressed by adding more structural support to your nose & by rearranging the tip cartilages. The fullness in the supratip (ie "pollybeak") can be improved - this is either remaining cartilage, a graft or scar tissue. Revision rhinoplasty is more difficult than the first time, but worth it if your concerns bother you. Good luck!

How long should I wait to have plastic surgery after gastric sleeve surgery?
Cosmetic surgery post gastric sleeve
Apr 12, 2015
If you are considering a cosmetic procedure after a gastric sleeve, you should wait until reaching maximal weight loss with maintenance of that particular weight. This is usually 12 to 18 months post op. It is very important that you take your vitamins as recommended by your bariatric surgeon and keep up with your daily protein requirement for healthy wound healing.

Conversion to a gastric bypass is a secondary option for treating acid reflux post sleeve. I would also rule out a sliding hiatal hernia a cause of your symtoms. Even a small hiatal hernia in the presence of a sleeve can cause severe reflux and a corrective operation can alleviate your symptoms.
Does gastric sleeve increase pre diagnosed acid reflux?
Acid Reflux post gastric sleeve
Apr 12, 2015
Acid reflux can be a debilitating side effect of a gastric sleeve. If you have significant acid reflux prior to a gastric sleeve, you need to consult with your bariatric surgeon and have a solid work up prior to your planned surgery. There are many variables that factor into your decision, therefore, it is important to have a detailed consultation with a qualified surgeon.

Lap Band Slipped, Could I Have the Sleeve Done?
Revision of a slipped Lap Band
Sep 20, 2013
Revision of a slipped lap band is an acceptable option and should be considered in a pt with recurrent obesity. Most of the time even with a minor slip, a surgeon can remove the lap band and perform a sleeve gastrectomy in the same setting. Repositioning of the lap band is usually not recommended. Depending on your BMI and co-morbidities your surgeon may choose a conservative approach rather than a revision to a sleeve.

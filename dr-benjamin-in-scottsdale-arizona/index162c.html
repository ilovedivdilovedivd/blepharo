Back or arm lipo? Which area should I do first?
Arm lipo
Sep 21, 2023
Hello! I think that most plastic surgeons that are skilled in performing arm liposuction would perform lipo360 of the arms with some sort of skin tightening modality (i.e. J plasma, body tight, etc). This would address the entirety of the arm including the back/posterior and give you better overall definition of the arms. Although you would benefit from full back liposuction as well, I would start with the lipo360 of the arms and after healed possibly address the back and torso. Good luck!

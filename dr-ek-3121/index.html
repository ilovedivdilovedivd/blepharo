What breast lift type do I need?
Breast Lift
Nov 14, 2018
I would suggest a vertical scar (lollipop) breast lift with auto-augmentation of the upper pole utilising tissue from the lower pole of the breast. This will improve your upper pole fullness and shape. If you’d like, this can be combined with fat grafting to improve overall shape and volume.
From a costs point of view, prices can vary from 11 – 15k depending on insurance coverage.
Best of luck

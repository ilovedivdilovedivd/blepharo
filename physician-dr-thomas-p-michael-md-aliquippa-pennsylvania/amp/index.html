Laser hair removal: should I avoid getting hair removal during the summer?
Laser Hair Removal in Summer
Jul 18, 2017
You can certainly have laser hair removal done in the summer/sunny months. However, you can not be actively tanning. We very much recommend that people begin laser hair removal in the fall months to avoid this issue. If however you are very cautious in the sun and are always shaded/wearing sun screen you can begin anytime of year.

Would I be a good candidate for a lip lift and how would it affect the corners of my lips? (Pho
Lip lift candidate
Aug 19, 2020
Yes, you would be a good candidate for a lip lift. As you are so astute to point out, the corners of your lips will appear to be hidden after the central lip is lifted with a traditional bullhorn lip lift procedure. A corner lip lift would need to be added to ensure the corners are lifted as well. This is done by removing skin from just above the upper lip border along the outside of the lips. It would be best to combine these two approaches together.

Lip lift
Jul 8, 2020

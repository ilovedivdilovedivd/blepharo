What is the standard eyelid surgery recovery time?
Eyelid surgery recovery time
Dec 19, 2019
There really isn’t a true standard recovery time as it varies from patient to patient. Most patients are back to work in 1-2 weeks, but I have found that depends on one’s comfort level of returning to work while still looking swollen and/or bruised. Sometimes, steroid creams with massage or injections of certain medications (steroid or 5FU) can help address certain scars.

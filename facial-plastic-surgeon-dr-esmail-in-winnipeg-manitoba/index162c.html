What is the typical rhinoplasty recovery like?
What is the typical rhinoplasty recovery like?
Mar 24, 2021
Recovery from a Rhinoplasty procedure is quite comfortable with most patients only requiring pain medication for the first 24hrs post procedure. Upon waking from surgery, your nose will be covered with a splint (which you may need to wear for up to one week). Expect to be swollen and possibly bruised around the eyes and cheek area. 80% of swelling is typically down at one month post procedure and the tip starts to show more refinement. How Much Does Rhinoplasty Cost?
How much does Rhinoplasty Cost?
Mar 24, 2021
The cost for a Surgical Rhinoplasty procedure typically ranges anywhere between $8,500 – $11,000+gst. Price varies depending on each patient’s individual needs and goals. The plastic surgeons credentials and equipment being used during the procedure may also affect the cost. It is recommended to meet in-person with the surgeon to share your goals, which will help determine the best procedure for you.

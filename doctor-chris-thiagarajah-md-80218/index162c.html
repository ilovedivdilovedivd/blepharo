Juvederm in tear trough. Is it normal to have a raised area after 12 days where the injection was
Not sure you need surgery
Nov 27, 2018
One week is pretty early. I use Restylane, Beletero and Juvederm Volbella in the tear trough. Regular Juvederm is not often used. I would give it one more week. Sometimes filling the tear trough can accentuate the flatness of the mid face and transition area. It may be fine in a week but one week is too early to judge.

9 months on and I still have double vision?
neuro-ophthalmologist
Nov 19, 2017
You need to see a neuro-ophthalmologist…someone who specializes in adult double vision. The botox is worn off so it may be something more serious.

I had Sculpsure today. I have been taking prednisone for 6 days prior to treatment. Will this
SculpSure
May 7, 2019
Since you finished the prednisone prior to the SculpSure, the anti-inflammatory effects of the prednisone should be minimal. A successful SculpSure treatment depends on the inflammatory response generated by destruction of the fat cells. That triggers the body to reabsorb the fat cells. You should try to avoid any further anti-inflammatory medications including NSAIDs such as ibuprofen and naproxen. I hope this helps.
Will I repeat my laser sessions after bikini hair is back after pregnancy?
Laser hair removal
May 7, 2019
The hormonal changes during pregnancy has likely contributed to the hair regrowth. Laser treatment after delivery should be effective again at removing that unwanted hair. You may or may not need as many treatments as before, depending on how much hair has regrown. Good luck with your pregnancy!

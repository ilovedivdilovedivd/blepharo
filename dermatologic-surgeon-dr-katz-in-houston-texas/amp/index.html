What is the best (and safe) treatment/laser for uneven tone and texture in dark skin?
LaseMD safety
Jun 3, 2021
You are correct that LaseMD is a safer choice for your skin type over IPL. But I would still make sure you are not tan at all and prep your skin with hydroquinone prior to and in between laser treatments. LaseMD works only only on hyperpigmentation with very little effect on collagen itself so it will likely not make your skin feel smoother but can help with the hyperpigmentation. Also, make sure you are a skin care regimen that targets pigment and use hydroquinone on and off.

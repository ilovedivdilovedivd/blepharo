Would you suggest a lower blepharoplasty?
Bags under the eyes
Feb 18, 2020
Thank you for your question and for providing the photos. I think you would be a good candidate for a lower blepharoplasty where your own fat is redistributed to smooth out your eyelid-cheek junction. This could be through the inside of the lower eyelid (transconjunctival incision), which would mean you wouldn’t have any scars on the lower eyelid itself.The key thing is to be assessed by an appropriately trained and experienced plastic surgeon.I hope this helps.

Vertical scar breast reduction
Feb 6, 2020
Thank you for your question and for providing the photos.There is always a limit to how mich tissue you can safely remove during a breast reduction.

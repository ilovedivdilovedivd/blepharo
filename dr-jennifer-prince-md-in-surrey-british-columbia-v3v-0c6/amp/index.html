Is it normal for my incision to be raised/hard 4 weeks post op Tummy tuck, MR and lip?
Scarring after tummy tuck
Aug 22, 2018
It is common for the scars to feel tender, raised and hard for the first 6 weeks after a tummy tuck. Usually after 6 weeks the scars will start to lighten and soften, but it takes up to a year for the scars to fully mature and to get the final scar result.

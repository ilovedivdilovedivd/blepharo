When should I feel relief from Botox injections? Had 4 Botox injections 3 days ago for TMJ relief.
Give It Time
Apr 16, 2018
Botox typically takes 5-10 days to kick in when used in the forehead or crow's feet. Because the masseter muscles (the ones that you use to chew, and the ones you are clenching due to your TMJ) are very large, it can take longer for the botox to affect them. I advise patients to wait 2 weeks to start feeling the effects of botox in the masseter muscles. You will start to see muscle atrophy (slimming due to lack of use) at around 4 weeks.

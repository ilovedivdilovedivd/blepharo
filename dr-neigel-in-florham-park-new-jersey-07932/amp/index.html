Does Restylane Refyne work on vertical lip wrinkles?
Restylane Refyne for Vertical Lip Lines
Oct 18, 2018
   Yes! Restylane Refyne is the best filler on the market for vertical lip lines.  The great thing about Refyne is it can be injected superficially without getting the Tyndall effect.  The Tyndall effect describes a blueish hue that can be seen in the skin when a deep filler is accidentally injected too superficially. You will receive a much better result in the vertical lip lines if a superficial filler is injected.  Thus, Refyne is the best for these wrinkles.  Good luck!
10 units of Xeomin for 11s 3 days ago, no change
Xeomin
Sep 26, 2018
    It typically takes up to 2 weeks to see the final result of any neurotoxin.  In addition, 10 units of Xeomin is a small amount.  We usually place about 20 units into the glabella to get rid of "the elevens."  I would recommend making a follow up appointment with your injector at the 2 week mark.  If you are still unsatisfied with your results, you may need more units injected into your glabella.  Good luck!
Can Sculptra be injected directly into the cheeks?
Sculptra to the Cheeks
Aug 23, 2018
   Yes, Sculptra can absolutely be injected directly into the cheeks.  We inject into the cheeks quite often.  Sculptra is great in the cheeks.  It does take about 6 weeks for the Sculptra to "fill-in".  You will see a nice difference.  If you are looking for instant gratification, we also suggest Resylane Lyft or Voluma to cheeks.  But, I would recommend waiting until you Sculptra has "filled-in," then reassess.  Good luck!

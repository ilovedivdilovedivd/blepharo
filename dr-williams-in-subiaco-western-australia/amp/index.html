10 days post op, will this improve?
10 days post op wounds.
Oct 10, 2014
Thank you for you question. Your wounds look from the photos to be healing well. It is a little difficult from the photo to see the detail. However your wounds and subsequent scars will improve significantly with time. For the next few weeks they may become a little thickened and red before settling down over 6 – 12 months. If you have any concerns in that time I would suggest you seeing your plastic surgeon.

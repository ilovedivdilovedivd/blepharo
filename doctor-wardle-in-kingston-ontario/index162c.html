Can I get breast implants while on Accutane?
Accutane
May 26, 2009
Patients on ACCUTANE considering either surgical procedures or topical skin treatments including laser or chemical peels are at higher risk of scarring than patients not on ACCUTANE.
It is recommended to be off ACCUTANE for a MINIMUM of 6 months before undergoing these treatments/surgery–1 year off would reduce risks further.

Realistic upper and lower bleph recovery timescale?
Recovery from eyelid surgery
Nov 29, 2018
Two weeks is a good estimate to be recovered that the average person wouldn’t notice that you recently had surgery.  For close friends, that spend significant time with a face to face conversations, the early healing scars or mild residual swelling may be noticeable for a month or so. Healing time varies from person to person. 
How Much Does a Lower Eye Lift Cost?
Cost of eyelid surgery
Nov 28, 2018
The cost of eyelid surgery can vary in different cities and across different practices.  In my practice, eyelid surgery is around $3500-$4700.  Upper and lower eyelid surgery together typically cost around $6000-$7000 plus facility and anesthesia fees.  Best of luck! 
Are my eyes too close together?
Eye distance
Nov 28, 2018
In evaluating your pictures, your eyes look to to be the normal distance apart and your face looks well balanced.  Selfies and FaceTime camera images can distort your features, so try not to utilize these tools to over analyze your features.  
Will the swelling on one side that is not moving go?
Swelling after surgery
Nov 19, 2018
It’s normal to still have swelling after surgery,even after 4 weeks. Everyone heals at different rates and there are many factors that influence swelling.  Discuss your concerns with your plastic surgeon.  
I am really insecure about my face profile! Which surgery do you I should do to improve it? (picture)
Facial balance
Nov 19, 2018
A few small changes to your face, would provide a more balanced profile.  Your chin is underprojected and a simple chin implant procedure could bring your chin into better alignment with your facial features.  A rhinoplasty to refine your nose and give less nostril show would also make your profile more elegant. Be sure to consult with a board certified and experienced surgeon to further discuss your goals.  
I had a lower blepharoplasty three weeks ago, are these bumps normal? When will they go away?
Bumps on incision line
Nov 19, 2018
It’s difficult to tell from the picture.  An in office evaluation with your surgeon is the best approach to better understand what the bumps are.  You are still in the early healing phase after your surgery.  Incision scars often soften and smooth out significantly after a few months but can often look the thickest 4-6weeks after surgery.  The bumps could be reactions from the sutures, it could be trapped skin or Milia , or just thicker early scar that will flatten and fade over time.  
Can I expect mini facelift final result to be same as day 1?
Swelling after surgery
Nov 16, 2018
Swelling is often worse day 2 and 3 after surgery than the first day when the dressing is removed. Peak swelling is often 48-72 hours after surgery.  The combination of fat transfer with facelift can cause significant swelling. Give yourself a few weeks to months to heal before evaluating your outcome.  Best of luck!

Michael A. Persky, MD

Name: Michael A. Persky, MD
Statement: Dr. Michael A. Persky is a board-certified facial plastic surgeon in Encino, California. With over 36 years in practice, he is highly sought after for his expertise in facial rejuvenation, including facelifts, neck lifts, rhinoplasty, chin surgery, and eyelid surgery.

He has also mastered skin rejuvenation and resurfacing via noninvasive and minimally invasive technologies and is an expert injector.

Dr. Persky is proud to have created a practice that offers the latest surgical and nonsurgical techniques and technologies, to rejuvenate the faces of men and women of all ages. He is a surgeon you can trust to help you achieve and, most importantly, maintain all of your facial aesthetic goals. “When you trust your face to us, you can trust that we will do our best and everything to help you achieve your aesthetic goals,” says Dr. Persky.

He earned his medical degree from Emory University School of Medicine in Atlanta, and then completed a residency in Otolaryngology - Head and Neck Surgery at the Los Angeles County + University of Southern California Medical Center in Los Angeles. Today, Dr. Persky is board certified by the American Board of Otolaryngology - Head and Neck Surgery.

Dr. Persky is also a medical expert and consultant for the media. He has appeared on many television programs and media outlets to discuss facial plastics, including the Los Angeles Times, Los Angeles Magazine, and the Daily News.

Dr. Persky has been practicing facial cosmetic and reconstructive surgery since 1985, in private practice at the same Encino, California location. His education, exceptional skills, knowledge, experience, and artistry allow him to provide his patients with exceptional results that are customized to their unique facial structure.

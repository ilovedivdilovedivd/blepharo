what is the best treatment for Under Eye Wrinkles?
Skin rejuvenation
May 27, 2014
It appears you have very large pores that could be contributing to your concerns about your skin. There is no laxity in the skin, which is not surprising at your age. I would recommend medical grade skin care with tretinoin/Retin-A and a laser resurfacing. The combination of those 2 treatments would greatly improve you skin texture.
How to fix an uneven jaw without surgery?
Consider filler
Apr 22, 2014
The picture is not great but I think I see what you are referring to. It appears that you have a more prominent depression just right of the chin. This is called the prejowl sulcus. A bit of filler, like Radiesse would likely greatly improve the appearance and lend more symmetry to your jawline.Landon McLain MD, DMD Huntsville, AL
Post-op: Are gum recession and infection common consequences of a dental implant surgery?
Periodontal disease
Apr 22, 2014
If you have an infection you should follow up with your dentist, periodontist or an oral and maxillofacial surgeon. Infection can lead to recession of the gums and loss of bone. This does not sound like malpractice though.Landon McLain MD, DMD Huntsville, AL
1 week post op chin implant. I can barely smile, I look very weird and can’t smile the way us
Don’t worry just yet
Apr 22, 2014
At 1 week there should be a fair amount of swelling remaining. I have found that it really takes 2-3 weeks for the majority of swelling to recede. Numbness to the lip this early is also not uncommon. You and your surgeon will be better able to assess things in another few weeks. Hope all goes well and you love your chin implant as most patients typically do. Landon McLain MD, DMD Huntsville, AL.

Nose/facial asymmetry – will rhinoplasty help?
List you priorities
Dec 5, 2020
Hi There, I can see the facial asymmetry and even though the nose appears displaced, the problem is not the nose, but the face itself. Trying to reposition your nose on the face could make matters worse.Facial symmetry could be improved both surgically or non-surgically with facial filler or fat grafting.The nose should can definitely be reshaped and refined but will not improve facial symmetry.The best thing is to have an assessment and discuss possible results and limitations.
7 months post rhinoplasty, damage/swelling still possible?
Nasal wash
Dec 5, 2020
Hi There, nasal swelling after rhinoplasty can take a long time to completely subside, especially on the nasal tip, it can take 12 – 18 months.Please stop picking your nose. It is possible to cause damage, even infection. You can wash you nose with saline twice a day if it is dry and crusty. Better to see you doctor.

Too much skin on nose for rhinoplasty?
Tension nose
Dec 5, 2020
Hi There, it seems like you have a tension nose and the cartilage overgrowth may have stretched the skin. As long as you are not a smoker and does not have other medical conditions that may impair healing, you should heal just fine.Hard to assess your skin over a photograph. Thick skin on the nasal tip may add to the bulk and may limit how much more refined the tip can be made.The best thing is to come for an assessment and have specialist advice.

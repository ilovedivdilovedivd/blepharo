Sanusi Umar, MD

Name: Sanusi Umar, MD
Statement: Based in Manhattan Beach, CA, Dr. Sanusi Umar, MD is a double board-certified dermatologist and hair transplant surgeon in the greater Los Angeles area, with nearly two decades of experience.

Inventor of the U.S. patented Dr. UGraft Zeus FUE hair transplant system, Dr. Umar specializes in the extraction of all donor hair types, including scalp, nape, and body hair. His use of non-head hair benefits previously excluded groups such as those with severe baldness, African Americans, and young patients.

Using state-of-the-art technology, Dr. Umar’s practice, the Dr. U Hair and Skin Clinic, also offers a wide range of solutions for dermatological concerns. Options include severe acne and acne scar treatments, keloid scar removal, Botox, lip augmentation, minimally invasive facial contouring, Fraxel laser resurfacing, and dermal fillers like Juvéderm, Radiesse, and Restylane.

Dr. Umar is also a leader in the removal of Acne Keloidalis Nuchae, or AKN. Additionally, he offers FaceTite, BodyTite, and AccuTite procedures from InMode; Evolve fat reduction and skin tightening, and Emsculpt muscle toning.

His clinic in Manhattan Beach also features some of the best regenerative medicine procedures available, including Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP), Nanofat grafting, and Exosomes.

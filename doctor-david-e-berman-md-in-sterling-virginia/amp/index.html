Will going from an increase in breast volume (A to C cup) lead to sagging in the future?
what sags is your natural breast tissue which is a function of your skin and tissue tone
Jan 25, 2023
Since your implants never sag, it is your natural skin and breast tissue that may sag with time. the size of the implants will not make this more likely. In fact probably selecting a slightly larger implant may help minimize some sagging of your breast tissue. however, for the most part, your skin and breast tissue are not affected by the implants.

david berman md, frcsc

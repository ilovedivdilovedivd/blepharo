Would Bellafill erase/improve my acne scars?
Bellafill could help!
Dec 13, 2021
Hi there! Bellafill can be used to fill atrophic acne scars, and from your photo it looks like this is what you have. (Atrophic scars can be identified as depressions in the skin.) We would recommend talking with your dermatologist to see if Bellafill would help improve your acne scars!
Does Bellafill not work for some people?
Hope this helps!
Dec 13, 2021
Hi there! Bellafill is a collagen stimulator, so we always recommend that our patients eat plenty of protein after their injections to get the best results. Some individuals can't eat a lot of protein because of other health related reasons, so if this is the case this may be why you are seeing little improvement. Is there any way to get Bellafill removed from my cheeks?
Yes, there is a way!
Dec 13, 2021
Hi there! Yes, there is a way to "remove" Bellafill from the cheeks. Your doctor should be able to inject small amounts of cortisone where you got the Bellafill. This will melt the filler away. Hope this helps!

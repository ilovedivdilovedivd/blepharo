How long should a person wait post rhinoplasty before doing a chemical peel or laser skin resurfaci
Chemical peel or laser post rhinoplasty
May 5, 2019
I would wait 2 months before doing a chemical peel or laser after rhinoplasty. At this stage, resurfacing will really not have a negative impact on your result or swelling.
Make sure you do not expose your incision site to any laser or chemical peel as this will likely interfere with your scar healing in this area.

Aslo, confirm that your surgeon is happy with this as he should really be informed of this if you decide to go ahead.

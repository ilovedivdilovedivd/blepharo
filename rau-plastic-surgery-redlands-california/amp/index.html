Todd C. Rau, MD

Name: Todd C. Rau, MD
Statement: Dr. Todd C. Rau, MD is a board-certified plastic surgeon with 16 years of experience. His well-established private practice serves patients from Redlands, San Bernardino, and Riverside, California and provides a range of plastic surgery procedures including breast augmentation, liposuction, rhinoplasty, tummy tuck, and breast lift. Dr. Rau prides himself on his reputation as a skilled surgeon and works hard to better the lives of all his patients.

Dr. Rau studied biological science at the University of California, Davis before receiving his medical degree at the American University of the Caribbean School of Medicine in Sint Maarten. He went on to complete a general surgery residency at St. Agnes Hospital (affiliated with The Johns Hopkins Hospital and the University of Maryland Medical System) in Baltimore, as well as a plastic surgery residency at Penn State Health Milton S. Hershey Medical Center.

Today he is an active member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.

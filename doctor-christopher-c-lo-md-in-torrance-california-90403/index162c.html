Fix blue/purple dark circle hue under and around eyes?
Darkness under eyes
Jun 7, 2023
The skin around the eyes is the thinnest on the body and you are seeing some of the normal blood vessels underneath the skin in this area. In addition to concealer, which can be applied to the area, eye creams with caffeine can sometimes help constrict these vessels slightly to improve some of the dark circles. Be well.
Botox for chronic migraine has made my eyebrows droop badly. Would a brow lift be suitable in thi
Botox
Jun 7, 2023
Botox in the forehead area, which treats migraines, can weaken the muscles lifting the brows and accentuate brow ptosis and dermatochalasis (excess skin on the upper eyelids). In certain cases a brow lift and blepharoplasty can be performed to prevent this occurrence after your injections. I recommend follow up with an oculofacial surgeon for in person measurements. Be well.
What can be done to improve my lumpy neck skin?
Platysma bands and scar tissue
Jun 7, 2023
On the image, there appears to be pronounced platysma bands (vertical and become more pronounced with grimace). These vertical platysma bands can be treated with botox, which will need to be repeated every 3-4 months.
The incision you have can further be treated with injections (kenalog, 5-fluorouracil). Laser is another potential treatment to minimize the scar appearance.
An in person exam with a specialist will be the next step.

Botox and filler after treatments

It is important to review with your provider. However, in my practice, I will generally have patients wait 1 month after their surgical procedures to have botox or fillers injected. Be well!
What are the options to treat Chalazion for 6 years old?
chalazia
Apr 10, 2023
The good news is most chalazia will resolve on their own– even some that have been present for a few months. The warm compresses and eyelid hygiene are still useful both in treating chalazia and preventing them from coming on. IPL is not recommended for kids. The injections are also tough to perform in for kids in this age group. Unfortunately surgery will likely be recommended– though if it is not a chalazion that is very noticeable, I would recommend watchful waiting as well. Take care…

Will waiting 13 weeks instead of 11 weeks for Botox make a significant difference?
Botox
Apr 10, 2023
Thank you for your question. Botox generally wears off between 3-4 months after treatment. As the treatment wears off, your muscles will regain strength and the wrinkles may reform. This generally does not happen overnight, but is something that gradually sets back in. I do not think that changing the injection date by two weeks will make a noticeable difference. Best of luck!

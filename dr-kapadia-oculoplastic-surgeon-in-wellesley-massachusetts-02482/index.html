Restylane Define hardening. Can the hard pocket be dissolved?
Hard lumps after fillers
Oct 15, 2020
Hard lumps after fillers are not normal and should be dissolved. These are likely your body mounting an immune response to the fillers called a granuloma. They are best treated with hyaluronidase injections to dissolve the filler. It often takes multiple rounds of dissolving to get rid of these.
If I had an autoimmune response to Sculptra, how likely to have one to Voluma?
immune response
Sep 15, 2020
Sculptra and Voluma are very different fillers from an immune standpoint and unlikely to have cross reactivity. That being said, if there were a problem, Voluma can be notoriously difficult to dissolve. If patients have concerns about immune reactions, I usually recommend using Restylane Lyft in the cheeks which also works well in this area and is much easier to dissolve if there are any issues.

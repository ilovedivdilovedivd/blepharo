PicoSure vs Fraxel Laser: which laser goes deeper under the skin?
Picosure on Brown Skin
Jan 15, 2018
I would be hesitant to use Fraxel on Brown skin.  That is why I acquired the Picosure device.  On brown skin with pigment issues, it’s not about how deep you go.  It’s about treating the pigment without causing worse hyperpigmentation.  I would feel very confident to treat your skin with Picosure.  I would hesitate with Fraxel.Dr. N

After a breast lift, when does the discomfort go away? 2 months post-op.
Recovery after breast lift
Jan 4, 2018
Great question!You are still in the early stages of the recovery process and will need to be patient at this point.  I would say that anywhere between 6 months and 12 months is when you will experience final results, softening, and relaxing of the breast.Dr. N
Can I just get a breast lift?
Breast lift with fat transfer the upper part
Jan 4, 2018
I would recommend a breast lift with fat transfer to the top of the breasts.  The breast lift will still leave you empty at the upper portion.  The fat injection will help fill that out without implants.
Do I need a breast lift or implants? Or both?
Implants vs lift
Jan 4, 2018
I would probably use some smooth implants on you alone.  I would save a lift for when your really need it.  Especially, with darker skin types, the scarring is unpredictable with the lift.
Dr. N
Can a lift be done while keeping implants?
Lift with existing implants
Jan 4, 2018
If there is nothing wrong with your implants and you are happy with the size, then it is possible to perform a lift and keep your implants.  I would order new ones as a surgeon just in case any trauma occurred to the implants during the lift surgery and just return them if they were not needed.Dr. Sheila
Would implants be enough, or should I get a breast lift also??
Implants vs lift
Jan 4, 2018
There are nuances to breast surgery that can only be fleshed out during your consultation.  Sometimes, a smooth implant can be placed and a lift can be avoided.  Other times, depending on your desired final outcome, a lift and implant is necessary.
Dr. N

Antibiotics after CoolSculpting

TCA Peel at home

Why doesn’t laser hair removal work for me?
Laser Hair Removal
Feb 19, 2020
You will need more sessions of treatment. I have seen many patients that needed upwards of 12-20 treatments to get a true reduction with lasting results. These treatments should be done 6 weeks apart. Waiting a full 2 months, especially if you are seeing growth is too long between treatments.
Although rare, it is possible to be resistant to treatment.

Hope this helps!
Dr. Leeman

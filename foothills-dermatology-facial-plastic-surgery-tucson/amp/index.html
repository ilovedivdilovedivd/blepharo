Doctors

Jerold Olson, MD

Name: Jerold Olson, MD
Statement: Dr. Jerold Olson, MD, is a highly experienced double board-certified facial plastic surgeon based in Tucson, Arizona, boasting an impressive 26 years of expertise. As the founder of Foothills Dermatology & Facial Plastic Surgery, his primary focus lies in the realm of cosmetic and functional rhinoplasty, commonly known as "nose jobs." Dr. Olson extends his comprehensive services to encompass a wide array of facial plastic surgery procedures, encompassing facelifts, neck lifts, blepharoplasty (eyelid lifts), ear surgery, and hair restoration.

Dr. Olson's educational journey commenced at the University of Arizona College of Medicine, followed by a residency in otolaryngology - head and neck surgery at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center in Dallas. He further honed his craft through a fellowship in facial plastic surgery at Hedgewood Surgical Center in New Orleans, Louisiana, marking him as Southern Arizona's pioneering fellowship-trained facial plastic surgeon.

With a commitment to excellence, Dr. Olson actively participates in the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (AAFPRS) and holds board certifications from both the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology - Head and Neck Surgery.

Does the scalp reattach to the skull after hairline lowering procedure?
Scalp healing
Aug 6, 2022
Hello,
The scalp is lifted in between 2 structures, the periosteum is the layer just over the bone and the galea. After the forehead advancement these 2 areas heal down to one another again. There is some numbness associated with this procedure but once this improves the scalp feels completely normal to patients.
How Should You Sleep and Take Care of Your Skin After a Facelift?
Best Sleeping Position & Best Skin Care Post Facelift
Mar 3, 2021
For the first 2 nights it is recommended you sleep with your head elevated to minimize swelling. You may use a travel pillow to support your neck or a soft towel. This will help decrease the amount of post-operative swelling while healing. You may shower approximately 48 hours after a facelift but it should be kept brief. Using a gentle face wash once a day will keep your skin clean until you are able to resume your skin care regimen.

Sun bathed three months after rhinoplasty, will this cause permanent swelling?
Swelling after rhinoplasty
Jul 15, 2018
Hello,For the first few months after rhinoplasty any activity that causes swelling of the nose will be  more pronounced than before surgery. This can include sun exposure.  Depending on time of day an hour or two of sun exposure can be significant. It is unlikely that you caused any long-term problems with your nose. Check in with your surgeon. Good luck.JO

Clicking after rhinoplasty
Jun 24, 2018
Hello,
As you know this problem can certainly happen when a columellar strut graft is placed but is a bit unusual without.  I suspect the clicking is related to the inferior aspect of the septum moving against the underlying bone.  Please get back to your surgeon and hopefully they can identify the culprit.  I think there might be a relatively easy fix for this issue if it does not improve on its own.  Good Luck!

Is there an immediate, no-downtime exfoliation treatment? I usually get VI peels, bu
Skin Treatment Downtime VS. Results
Aug 10, 2023
As with many things in life you get out of a skin treatment what you put into it. That means in order to achieve greater results you should expect greater downtime. For example, microdermabrasion exfoliates and has no significant downtime. A phenol Croton oil peel has about 7 to 10 days for healing, but erythema that can last two or even 3 months. You can certainly find alternatives that have less downtime, but you should expect that the results equally would be diminished.
Will high profile or moderate profile change cup sized if they’re the same size in v
Breast implant profile and bra cup size
Aug 9, 2023
In short, no. Different profiles will not result in different cup sizes. In general, I would consider the volume of the implant in addition to the volume of your breast to correspond with a cup size. Selecting a certain volume will give the cup size, and then the decision between profiles is made based on the width of your breast base. A narrower individual will require a higher profile in order to minimize projection of the implant to the side.

When can I return to the gym after a facelift?
Recovery after facelift
Aug 9, 2023
I recommend for my patients to take one week of resting at home, and return to the gym 4 weeks after surgery. Walking as much as you wish can be begin right away. Your surgeon might allow you to begin light aerobic activity 2 weeks after surgery, but it’s important not to get your blood pressure up as this could cause bleeding or bruising.

Can I get one veneer to fix this small gap in my teeth?
Too Large for a Veneer
Jul 18, 2018
Unfortunately that space is too large for a veneer or a crown to correct. It would be very wide compared to the neighboring teeth and you would not appreciate the appearance. It might be possible to improve the appearance if you opted to crown several of your upper teeth allowing several crowns to be made sharing the job of filling the space and being made in proportion to the one that would fill that space

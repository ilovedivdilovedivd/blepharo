Statement: Dr. Jamil Ahmad is a certified plastic surgeon serving patients in the Greater Toronto Area. Dr. Ahmad is recognized for his expertise in aesthetic surgery for the face, breasts, and body. With precision and artistry, he specializes in rhinoplasty, mommy makeovers, and tummy tucks.

Dr. Ahmad earned his medical degree from the Royal College of Surgeons in Ireland, and then completed his residency in plastic surgery at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center in Dallas. He went on to a fellowship in breast reconstruction with the University of Toronto’s division of plastic and reconstructive surgery. He obtained his certification in plastic surgery from the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada.

Passionate about research and education, Dr. Ahmad is a frequent guest lecturer, as well as a presenter at plastic surgery meetings for professional organizations around the world. He is honored to have been a visiting professor for multiple esteemed universities, including Stanford University. In just over 11 years of practice, Dr. Ahmad has authored more than 120 articles and chapters published in prestigious medical journals. He has also co-authored two definitive textbooks on rhinoplasty.

His expertise in rhinoplasty secured Dr. Ahmad the first-ever appointment of a Canadian physician to the board of directors of the Rhinoplasty Society. He is also a member of the board of directors for the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery—only the second Canadian in the society’s history to have received this honor.

Dr. Ahmad is currently an assistant professor at the University of Toronto within the division of plastic and reconstructive surgery, where he directs the aesthetic surgery clinic’s residency program.

Doctors

Daniel E. Rousso, MD

Name: Daniel E. Rousso, MD
Statement: With over 30 years of experience, Dr. Daniel Rousso is a triple-board-certified facial plastic surgeon and the founder of Rousso Adams Plastic Surgery in Birmingham, Alabama. He performs an assortment of surgical procedures, such as face lifts, blepharoplasty, rhinoplasty, lip enhancement, and hair transplant procedures. Dr. Rousso also offers non-invasive treatments like laser skin resurfacing, dermal fillers, and Botox injections. All treatments are performed at his AAAHC-accredited facility to maintain the highest levels of patient safety, care, and satisfaction.

Dr. Rousso is recognized by patients and peers alike for his skilled hand and aesthetic eye, which deliver natural, beautiful results that improve his patients’ self-esteem and quality of life. He has participated in hundreds of seminars, authored numerous plastic surgery publications, and has served as a clinical assistant professor at Birmingham’s University of Alabama.

Dr. Rousso holds board certifications in facial and reconstructive Surgery, otolaryngology-head and neck surgery, and hair restoration. He maintains active membership in multiple professional organizations and has held the title of president for both the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery.

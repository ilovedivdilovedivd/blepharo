Jaw reduction adverse affects?
Jaw reduction adverse effects
Oct 20, 2020
From the photos that you have provided, i believe your problem is mostly soft tissue. If you have a jaw reduction surgery, this will undoubtedly lead to sagging of your skin and soft tissues. You may benefit from a jawline liposuction and buccal fat removal. However, nothing can substitute a proper in person evaluation and appropriate X rays.
Can custom jaw implants give a lifting effect and tighten the skin?
Jaw Reduction leading to skin laxity
Oct 20, 2020
A jaw reduction can lead to skin laxity even in young patients. If you desire to restore your original jaw shape then implants may be indicated but if there is skin laxity then you may benefit from a lift procedure.
How soon can I have jaw surgery for my underbite after having Kybella treatment done?
Jaw surgery after Kybella
Oct 20, 2020
As long as you do not have any untoward reaction from the injections, you may proceed with jaw surgery anytime.
Is it possible to get a sliding genioplasty and jaw reduction at the same time?
Jaw reduction and a sliding genioplasty
Oct 20, 2020
Yes these two procedures are performed simultaneously all the time in my practice. They should be performed at the same time so that there will be structural balance to your face and jaws.
Do Chin and Cheek Reductions Come with Any Dangers?
Chin and Cheek reduction come with dangers
Oct 20, 2020
Yes, any surgery can lead to potential complications. Chin surgery can lead to numbness of the lip and chin which can be either temporary or permanent whereas cheek reduction surgery can lead to asymmetry and other complications. These are procedures that are performed all the time in my practice however we can not guarantee that these procedures are not without complications.
Would I be a good candidate for jaw reduction?
Good candidate for jaw reduction
Oct 20, 2020
Whether you are a good candidate for surgery can only be determined after an in person examination. However from the photos it seems that you have a very strong jaw, jaw muscle (Masseter) and chin. Under these circumstances I usually recommend a genioplasty to narrow the chin and a jaw reduction which are all performed through the mouth. Also depending on your age and elasticity of your skin you may require at the minimum liposuction of the jawline and neck to tighten the overlying skin….
Jaw reduction/lip lift: would a jaw reduction to feminize my face be possible?
Jaw Reduction and lip lift
Oct 20, 2020
I believe you are aiming for a V line which is a procedure we perform in our office all the time. The jaw is not shaved, the jaw is cut from the angle down to the premolar region to allow for a smooth transition, and then the chin is narrowed by performing a T genioplasty.

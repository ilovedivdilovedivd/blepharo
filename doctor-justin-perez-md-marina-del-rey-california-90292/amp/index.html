9 months post-op facelift – what exactly is wrong with my neck?
Residual neck fullness after facelift
Jan 23, 2022
There are several structures that can cause fullness in the neck- excess skin, subcutaneous fat, muscle, submandibular glands, and digastric muscles. As far as I can see, the prominent structures visible are a combination of submandibular gland and digastric muscle. In select cases, with those surgeons who are comfortable with this technique, those deeper structures can be partly removed (or suspended) to improve the fullness in this area.
Best,Dr. Perez

I have recently lost a lot of weight. As a result my neck looks awful!
Saggy neck skin after weight loss
Jun 24, 2016
The best way to improve your saggy neck skin is to have a lower face and neck lift. We all start to develop poor skin elasticity that easily stretches as we age. You’ve also lost facial volume that results in jowls that hang below your jawline. This can be corrected with fillers or your own fat. You are the ideal candidate, as long as your health permits, to correct this with surgery. Be sure to consult a board certified facial plastic or general plastic surgeon. Best of luck!

Jun 24, 2016

There are several options besides fillers that can be considered such as ultrasound or radiofrequency skin tightening although the surgical option will give the best result. You should consult a board certified plastic or facial plastic surgeon in your area who does both. Good luck!

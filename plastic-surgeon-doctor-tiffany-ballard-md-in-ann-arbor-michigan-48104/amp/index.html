How long typically can headaches last after Dysport injections?
Headache following Neuromodulator
May 6, 2018
Headaches after treatment with a neuromodulator such as Botox or Dysport typically last a few hours to a few days. You may treat your headache with a pain reliever like Tylenol or Advil; take the medication as directed for relief until it resolves. The result of the neuromodulator itself may take up to 2 weeks for full effect.
Is it risky to get lip filler if you have a cold sore?
Lip Filler with Cold Sore Present
Apr 30, 2018
Thank you for your question. I do not inject lip filler in the setting of an active cold sore and recommend rescheduling your appointment and waiting until it is fully resolved. In addition, if you have a history of cold sores, your provider will likely prescribe a course of a prophylactic anti-viral medication (Valtrex) to take when you have lip filler performed to reduce the risk of a cold sore developing.

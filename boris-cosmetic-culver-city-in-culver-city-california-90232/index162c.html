How many mm of nasal nostril could be reduced from my nose?
#nostrilWidthReduction
Mar 14, 2019
When I read your post I didn’t believe it but looking at the photo it does look 6cm wide at the alar base. The desired size is usually equal to the distance between the inner and outer corners of each eye (the intercanthal distance normally is 3.2cm). That would require an alar base reduction of 1.4cm from each nostril rim. That is more than usual so I suspect you will also need some thinning of the nostril rims in order to decrease the resection size to avoid a pinched nostril appearance.
Can blepharoplasty procedure be done with someone who has permanent eyeliner?
eye liner and blepharoplasty
Jan 16, 2019
The blepharoplasty incisions in the upper eyelid are in the crease and in the lower lid just below the eyelashes or on the inner surface. Since the tattoos are near the eyelashes they aren’t significant for upper blepharoplasties though they make look wider after surgery. For the lower eyelids if the incision is made on the inner surface the tattoo is irrelevant. If the incision is on the lower eyelid skin it may be okay depending on the width of the tattoo.

What kind of facelift do I need?
Facelift Type
Oct 13, 2023
If you’re interested in a durable and natural facelift, I strongly believe the deep plane facelift is an excellent option. This technique releases the deeper tissues that are actually descending with time as well as key tether points in the face. In addition there is no tension with closure, which provides an impactful result without any pulled appearance. The main areas it addresses are midface, jawline, and neck. I hope this helps and best of luck in your journey.

Is a corticosteroid nasal spray safe to use prior to rhinoplasty?
Flonase prior to Rhinoplasty
Oct 4, 2023
Great question and every surgeon has somewhat varying opinions on exactly when to stop steroid nasal sprays prior to surgery. Typically, I recommend stopping these 1 month prior to surgery to avoid nasal dryness and bleeding during and after surgery. Flonase can be taken daily for years without any systemic side effects so continuous use is reasonable for treatment of allergies otherwise.

Doctors

Steven K. Struck, MD

Name: Steven K. Struck, MD
Statement: Dr. Steven K. Struck is a Stanford-educated, board-certified plastic surgeon who has served the San Francisco Bay Area since 1998. At his practices in Atherton, Los Gatos, and Morgan Hill, California. Dr. Struck specializes in surgical procedures of the breast, body, and face. He’s also considered a pioneer at the forefront of skin laser technology. He embraces the latest in medical technology and innovation, often individualizing treatment by combining lasers with surgical techniques to achieve a youthful, natural-looking result.

Dr. Struck conducts personalized consultations to best determine a patient’s goals and expectations, make recommendations, and answer their questions in-depth. He offers a full complement of plastic surgery procedures, including facelifts, blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery), abdominoplasty (tummy tuck), breast augmentation, and liposuction (including Smart Lipo).

He’s passionate about creating youthful skin to complement his surgical results, and—along with a skin rejuvenation specialist—provides an array of nonsurgical treatments like Botox, PhotoFacial, Fraxel laser resurfacing, Cellulaze laser cellulite treatment, and dermal fillers, such as Juvéderm and Radiesse.

Dr. Struck received his medical degree from the University of Illinois College of Medicine. He completed an internship, as well as residencies in general surgery and plastic surgery, at Stanford University Hospital. He is an adjunct clinical faculty member in the department of plastic surgery, at Stanford University, where he teaches and supervises surgical procedures done by the residents.

Dr. Struck is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons. He has been published in medical journals, participated in research studies, and presented at medical conferences throughout the country.

Is it possible to have a beard to scalp transplant 3 months after FUE rather than waiting a year?
Rushing is not recommended
Sep 29, 2023
The main reason we wait about 1 year between grafts is that some of the hair follicles that were transplanted would not start to grow until 1 year after transplant. By waiting we ensure that we don’t accidentally damage these dormant follicles and allow you to get the most density possible. If the locations of the graft are different then you can have procedures closer to each other but if the location of the grafts overlap then I highly recommend that you wait.

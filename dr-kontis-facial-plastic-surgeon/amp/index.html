What will help straighten a crooked nose s/p revision?
Hold on--you're only 1 week postop!!!
Feb 25, 2020
Most patients don't realize that they are still swollen at one week when the dressing comes off. You may actually be swollen for 6-12 months--meaning it may take that long for the final result to become apparant. I would NOT suggest doing fillers at this time! Your surgeon is 100% correct that you should wait before doing anything. You can certainly have fillers to give an appearance of a straighter dorsum, but it may improve with time.

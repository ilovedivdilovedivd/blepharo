Scabs after Hair transplant
Apr 6, 2018
The black scabs are expected after Hair transplant. They typically fall off 5-10 days after the procedure. I advise against removing them yourself as that may pull out the transplanted grafts. Taking warm showers,without letting the shower stream hit the transplanted area directly, will help loosen the scabs and make them fall off faster.

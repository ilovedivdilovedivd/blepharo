Unhappy with nose 6 months post rhino, in your opinion is it botched?
Revision rhino
Apr 25, 2023
The key issue when it comes to revision is “Is my nose worse than it started?”. If it’s not, and yours definitely isn’t, revision is not a good idea. It may not be a bad idea, but it’s definitely not a good one. You need to wait until 12 months, and talk to your surgeon. And stop wasting your mental energy thinking about it until then, it’s not useful.

When is it safe to have Emsculpt after gallbladder surgery?
Emsculpt after laparoscopic surgery
Jun 26, 2021
I would wait at least 6 weeks after any abdominal surgery and longer if you have any pain at any incision site especially the umbilical site. If there is any chance you may have a hernia at an incision site you should not perform Emsculpt until that incision/hernia is repaired. If you have any questions about your incision site I would contact your surgeon.
Emsculpt Neo - Time between treatments?
Emsculpt Neo time between treatments
Jun 26, 2021
For the plain Emsculpt you would need at least 2 days between treatments for your muscles to recover. With the new Emsculpt Neo which combines both HIFEM and RF you would need at least 5-10 days to recover. In our practice we perform one treatment a week. This is the usual timeframe for our RF devices as well. This is what I would recommend for both the muscles to recover and to maximize the RF treatments. Hope that helps!

80+ lb weight loss. What procedures should I consider?
Start with an extended abdominoplasty
Jul 21, 2023
With that amount of loose skin consider starting with an extended abdominoplasty that will contour the hips as well. Alternatives include a circumferential body lift
Breast lift and augmention is unsafe?
It’s safer to stage them
Jul 21, 2023
Breast augmentation and lift are routinely performed together however combining the two has a much higher complication rate. In patients with lax skin, it’s much better to stage the two operations
I only want breast augmentation without a lift
If most think you need a lift do it
Jul 13, 2023
If you have breast ptosis and perform a breast augmentation alone, it’s possible the implant will sit too low or the breast will drop off the implant. A very small amount of ptosis can be corrected with a highly cohesive implant places in the sub glandular plane along with a circumaerolar mastopexy (where the scar hides around the aerola)
Tummy tuck in a few months. What can I do to prevent “dog ears” from developing during recovery?
Careful measurement
Dec 7, 2022
And abdominoplasty surgery requires removing an ellipse of skin, if Measured perfectly, there should be no standing cone deformity/dog year
Time Sensitive: Am I a good candidate for TT?
Ideal weight
Dec 7, 2022
It’s best to be at your ideal weight prior to abdominal plasty surgery. That being said, you could continue to lose a small amount of weight postoperatively without a significant amount of loose skin. As it stands, you pretty be a good candidate for abdominal plasty surgery provided most of the central abdominal tissue is due to muscle separation as opposed to intraperitoneal fat
I’m 8 months postpartum – mini tummy tuck or full?
Wait longer
Dec 7, 2022
Your body goes through a lot of hormonal changes, post pregnancy, and that results in significant weight changes. Wait until approximately one year prior to having an abdominal plasty. I do think you’ll require an abdominal plasty as it appears to be both separation of the muscles as well as loose skin.
Would a fleur de lis tummy tuck yield the best results?
Fleur-de-lis
Dec 7, 2022
Given the prominent central abdominal scar, a fleur-de-lis, abdominal plasty would make a lot of sense, and would excised tissue both vertically, and will excised the pre-existing scar to make it thinner and more aesthetically appealing

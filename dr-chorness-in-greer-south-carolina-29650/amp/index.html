Dysport Vs. Botox – What’s the Difference?
Dysport and Botox
Feb 4, 2019
Dysport and Botox are both derivatives of botulinum toxins type A proteins and work essentially the same. Dysport have smaller protein particles, therefore, it will “kick in” slightly faster than Botox. Both last approximately the same amount of time (3-4 months) depending on amount and depth of injection. Price varies, but Dysport may be slightly less expensive than Botox.
I had both Scuplta and Dysport injected 3 days ago. Should I massage both areas?
massage vs. no massage
Feb 4, 2019
It best NOT to massage after Dysport injections for at least the first 4 hrs after being injected. Massaging can actually displace the dysport. On the other hand it is very important to massage the Sculptra. The Sculptra should be massaged 5 times a day, for 5 minutes, for 5 days.
Does Restylane Refyne work on vertical lip wrinkles?
Restylane Refyne for lip lines
Jan 23, 2019
Restylane Refyne is my favorite to use for the vertical lip lines. It is a flexible filler that moves naturally as your mouth moves. It is injected superficially to correct the vertical lines and does not cause the bluish tent (Tyndall affect) that other fillers may cause. Restylane Refyne can take years off by correcting those vertical lip lines.

Nov 9, 2022
Often after a breast augmentation the implants ride high and it can take up to six months for them to drop. You didn't mention exactly how long ago the surgery was but if it was less than six months ago, wait for the implants to drop, this can be facilitated by wearing a pectoral strap around the chest and upper poles of your breasts.

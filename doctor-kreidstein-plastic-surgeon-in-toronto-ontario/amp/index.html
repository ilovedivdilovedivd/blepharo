80+ lb weight loss. What procedures should I consider?
Limits of abdominoplasty
Jul 11, 2023
Congratulations on your weight loss. You are fortunate in that you have a narrow waistline that will show well with removal of your excess skin. An abdominoplasty will correct skin excess on the front of your abdomen, but a lower body lift that extends 360 d. would enable us to improve your sides and backside. Be sure to see a surgeon who has experience with belt lipectomy or lower body lift.

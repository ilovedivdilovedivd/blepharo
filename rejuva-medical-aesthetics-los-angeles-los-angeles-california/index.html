Kian Karimi, MD, FACS

Name: Kian Karimi, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Kian Karimi, MD, FACS is a facial plastic surgeon in Los Angeles, California. He is double board-certified by the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology - Head & Neck Surgery. One of the country’s top facial plastic surgeons, he has extensive knowledge and experience in both surgical and non-invasive cosmetic procedures, which he offers with his team of expert medical professionals at Rejuva Medical Aesthetics, his premier private practice.

After graduating magna cum laude from the University of Indiana, he received his medical degree from Indiana University School of Medicine, where he was inducted into the Alpha Omega Alpha honor society, a distinction reserved for the top 10% of medical students in the country. Dr. Karimi then completed his residency in Head and Neck Surgery at the University of Florida performing over 2000 procedures during his term. Under the tutelage of Dr. Peter Adamson, the former President of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (AAFPRS), Dr. Karimi completed his fellowship in facial plastic and reconstructive surgery.

At his Brentwood, Los Angeles aesthetic practice, Dr. Karimi and his team offer patients a variety of cosmetic procedures, from facial plastic surgery to cutting-edge nonsurgical treatment modalities including state-of-the-art energy-based devices.

Recognized as a pioneer in noninvasive procedures, Dr. Karimi personally trains surgeons across the nation on best practices of PDO thread lifts, microcannula dermal filler injection techniques, and utilization of autologous blood plasma for aesthetic and reconstructive purposes.

Dr. Karimi has received numerous prestigious awards ranging from the Kris Conrad Merit Award for Outstanding Achievements in Facial Plastic Surgery, as well as being nationally recognized for his research on “Ethical Considerations in Aesthetic Rhinoplasty.”

He has contributed to media outlets for his innovative aesthetic techniques and has been recurrently featured on CBS, The Doctors TV and Extra! For nine consecutive seasons, Dr. Karimi served as the plastic surgeon for the Los Angeles Kings Hockey organization and is a proud Stanley Cup Champion. He is also a volunteer professor at UCLA’s School of Medicine and USC Keck School of Medicine.

Dr. Karimi is trilingual, speaking English, Spanish, and Farsi fluently.

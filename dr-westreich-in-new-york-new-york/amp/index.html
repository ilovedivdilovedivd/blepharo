Is there a procedure to get rid of fat lines on neck?
Neck lines aka “Tech neck”
Sep 23, 2019
Nowadays, younger people have more neck lines due to the constant neck flexion that occurs when looking at personal electronic devices. This has become known as “tech neck.” Typically our recommendations are to perform a baseline skin tightening with fractora or Morpheus. This helps for long term correction. If lines are still visible then filler is used to selectively smooth the lines. 
Anyway to improve eyebrow droop from Botox?
Droopy brows from botox
Aug 15, 2019
Eyebrow can drop from too much forehead botox or forehead botox placed too low. The only way to raise the brow is towards the side of the brow. Injecting the orbicularis below the brow can give 1-3mm of elevation. But it will give an arched brow which some patients don’t want. Alternatively, botox begins to wear off even at a month so brow dropping usually only lasts 4-6 weeks, not 4 months. This is especially true if you still have movement.

Changing your face shape and asymmetry

Could I have a closed rhino or will a nose filler look good with my nose?
Liquid versus surgical Rhinoplasty
Jul 15, 2019
Liquid or 'non-surgical rhinoplasty' involves using filler to smooth out and build up the nose, making a bump disappear on the bridge or have the nose look narrower from the front. The best candidates are those with flat or small noses that need additional height in their bridge. When the nose has a very tall profile, surgical Rhinoplasty is a much better option, since it makes the nose smaller, not bigger.

What does a “lateral” under eye filler entail and what type of filler should be used?
Lateral Orbit Fillers
May 6, 2019
Many people talk about the hollows under the eyes (or nasojugal crease) as an area for fillers. That is near the center of the eye near the nose. The lateral hollows are on the other side of the eye, towards the cheekbone. Hollows there are just as important for a youthful eye and go along with a high cheekbone. I tell my patients that hollows near the center make you look tired, but lateral hollows make you loom old. Both can be addressed with fillers.

Which procedure would help correct my uneven eyelids?

Confusion about what a mullerectomy is based on web searches
Mullerectomy
Oct 17, 2023
The Muller’s muscle can be manipulated to elevate or lower the eyelid.
Typically, a Mullerectomy (or internal ptosis repair) is used to raise a droopy upper lid. During this procedure the upper lid everted, conjunctiva and Muller’s muscle is excised, and sutures are then placed. This effectively tightens Muller’s muscle and raises the lid.
For upper lid retraction repair, Muller’s muscle is disinserted from the tarsus to help lower lid.
Best to see an eyelid expert.

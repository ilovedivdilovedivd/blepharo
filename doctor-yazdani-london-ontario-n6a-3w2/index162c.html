Breast augmentation – before or after kids? However I am trying to decide if I should have one now o
Breast Augmentation post pregnancy
May 1, 2018
If you are planning on having more children then you should delay getting breast augmentation. Pregnancy and more specifically, breast feeding, can change the breast shape even after surgery. This can lead to a re-operation. It is best to wait until you are finished pregnancy and at least 3-6 months after completing breast feeding so that you minimize the risk of needing a re-operation.
Hope this helps.

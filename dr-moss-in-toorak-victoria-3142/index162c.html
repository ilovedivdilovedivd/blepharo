What treatment would you recommend for chemosis?
Post-operative Chemosis
Jul 5, 2022
Post-operative Chemosis is swelling and inflammation of the conjunctiva after eyelid surgery. Just like the eyelids swell after eyelid surgery, the conjunctiva can also swell. The conjunctiva is the clear membrane that coats your eye’s outer surface. Most of the time chemosis occurs in a small area on the outside corner of the sclera (the white part of your eye).

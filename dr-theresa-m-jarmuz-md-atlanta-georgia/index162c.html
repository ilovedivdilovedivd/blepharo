Sculptra for someone with Multiple Sclerosis?
Sculptra
Apr 9, 2022
There are many factors here. 1) Sculptra creates a controlled inflammation - If you are any medications that could suppress this, your result may be impaired. 2) It is known that any event that the body perceives as a trauma - liposuction - can exacerbate MS. 3) I would not do anything without discussing with your Neurologist. At least, take the time to gather some information from your Doctor who is managing your MS.
J Scaljon

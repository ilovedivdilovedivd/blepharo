Can someone get pseudostrabismus from upper and lower lid surgery ?
Yes
May 14, 2019
Broadly speaking, restrictive strabismus (eye motility problems due to scar tether) can occur with eyelid surgery. Without seeing your photos, it’s hard to tell whether you have pseudo-strabismus (the appearance of eye motility/alignment problems usually due to the eyelid anatomy). If you suspect a problem with your eye movement, and especially if you have double vision, you should seek care from your surgeon immediately.

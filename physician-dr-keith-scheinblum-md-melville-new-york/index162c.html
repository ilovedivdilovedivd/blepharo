PRP for surgical/traumatic hair loss?
Prp hair injections
Dec 13, 2020
Prp hair injections help stimulate hair follicles to grow. Injections work best on patients with thinning hair or new hair loss , so it seems like you would be a good candidate. Once the area of concern is “shiny”, the injections will most likely be less effective .
How Long Before Botox Results Can Be Seen?
Botox
Nov 1, 2019
Most patients will see their Botox start to work within 2-3 days after injection, with the maximum benefit at 2 weeks. There is no limit on how long one can keep getting Botox. The average patient likes a touch up every 3-4 months, but some people find the benefits can last up to 6 months. It really varies upon the individual.
Does microneedling and PRP increase hair growth in a predominant bald area?
Prp
Oct 28, 2019
Prp injections and microneedling work best with people at the early stages of hair loss and thinning hair. If the area is “shiny” and lacking any active hair follicles, Prp will not be effective.
How long does the UltraSlim procedure last and how many treatments are needed?
UltraSlim
Oct 25, 2019
The amount of time needed to complete a single treatment varies on the amount of areas being treated . Each area is treated for 8 minutes, sometimes longer if there is resistant visceral fat. The amount of treatments needed will vary and depend on your desired treatment goals.
Does the PRP treatment works for areas that does not have any hair follicles?
Prp
Oct 25, 2019
Platelet rich plasma injections (Prp) require active hair follicles to stimulate growth. If the area is “shiny” and smooth that usually indicates that there are no active hair follicles.

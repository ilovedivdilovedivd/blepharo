Statement: Dr. Manu Gujrati is a double board-certified facial plastic surgeon and hair restoration specialist in San Francisco, California, with more than a decade in practice. Affectionately known by his patients as Dr. G, he works alongside Dr. Parsa Mohebi at Parsa Mohebi Hair Restoration, providing a robust range of services and modern techniques to treat men’s hair loss and women’s hair loss.

His specialties include follicular unit extraction and transplantation (aka FUE hair transplant), hair transplant repair, female hairline lowering, and effective treatments for concerns such as telogen effluvium, alopecia, trichotillomania, and anagen effluvium.

In addition to addressing hair loss on the scalp, Dr. Gujrati is skilled in beard hair transplant, eyebrow transplants, scalp scar revisions, transgender hair transplants, body hair transplants, and more.

Dr. Gujrati is eager to help provide education to his patients so they can better manage hair loss conditions and make the decisions that best suit their needs.

“I believe in offering expertise, education, experience and honesty to all my patients,” he says. “This is how our team provides genuine and true care for each individual that comes through our clinic. Your results should be both full and completely natural looking. That is the true art of hair surgery.”

In his undergraduate studies, Dr. Gujrati double majored in both Molecular and Cellular Biology, as well as Philosophy. He then went on to earn his medical degree at Northwestern University Feinberg School of Medicine in Chicago. Upon completion, he pursued a residency in Otolaryngology at the University of Kentucky in Lexington and was then accepted into the prestigious dual-fellowship of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery at the Rousso Facial Plastic Surgery Center and The Alabama Hair Institute in Birmingham, Alabama.

Today, Dr. Gujrati is double board-certified by the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery. He is a member of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (AAFPRS) and a fellow of the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery (ISHRS).

Dr. Gujrati has been the proud recipient of numerous honors and awards celebrating his hair restoration research and education efforts. He has also been closely involved with scientific exploration and meetings regarding hair loss on an international scale.

Helping his San Francisco hair restoration patients is Dr. Gujrati’s passion. Since the beginning of his career, he has attracted loyal clientele who’ve described him as having a “warm and friendly personality,” “comforting and generous with his time to educate,” and a “confident and precise” treatment technique.

With every hair restoration procedure performed, Dr. Gujrati combines artistic vision with his exemplary surgical skills to create a hairline that is both natural looking and long-lasting.

Whether you simply have questions, want to learn more about the different procedures offered, or want to jump into your hair restoration journey, Dr. Gujrati is eager to meet with you. Schedule a consultation to begin the conversation.

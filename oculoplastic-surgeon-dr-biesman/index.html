Statement: Dr. Brian S. Biesman is a board-certified oculoplastic surgeon practicing in Nashville, Tennessee. He specializes in oculofacial plastic surgery procedures such as eyelid surgery and brow lifts, as well as nonsurgical facial rejuvenation and CoolSculpting.

At his state-of-the-art facility, Dr. Biesman and his staff offer cutting-edge treatment options, with a mission to deliver skilled, ethical, and comprehensive care with personalized service. Along with body contouring and fat reduction treatments like CoolSculpting and Kybella, Dr. Biesman’s patients benefit from skin tightening treatments like Thermage and Ultherapy, wrinkle treatments such as Botox and Dysport, and dermal fillers like Juvéderm and Restylane.

After earning his undergraduate and medical degrees at the University of Michigan, where he graduated with high honors, Dr. Biesman completed his ophthalmology residency training at the Illinois Eye and Ear Infirmary. This was followed by fellowship training in ophthalmic plastic and reconstructive surgery at the prestigious Manhattan Eye, Ear and Throat Hospital in New York City.

Dr. Biesman then moved to Boston to assume the role of director of the Eye Plastics and Orbit Service and the Center for Aesthetic Eyelid and Facial Surgery at the New England Medical Center (now Tufts Medical Center). He was also an assistant professor of ophthalmology at Tufts University School of Medicine. During his time in Boston, Dr. Biesman also held faculty roles in the division of otolaryngology at the New England Medical Center and the divisions of dermatology and plastic and reconstructive surgery at the Beth Israel-Deaconess Medical Center.

Dr. Biesman is board-certified by the American Board of Ophthalmology. He is also a member of the American Society for Laser Medicine and Surgery, where he has held the roles of president, vice president, and laser safety officer. He is also a member of the American Society of Dermatologic Surgery, the American Society of Ophthalmic Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, the American Academy of Ophthalmology, the Tennessee Society for Laser Medicine and Surgery, and the American Medical Association.

Dr. Biesman has been recognized for his many contributions to the medical field, and has been the recipient of the Henry Baylis Lectureship Award from the American Society of Ophthalmic Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, as well as the Ellet H. Drake Memorial Award by the ASLMS.

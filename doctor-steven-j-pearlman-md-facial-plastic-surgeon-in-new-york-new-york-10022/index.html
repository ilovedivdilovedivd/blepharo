Raising bridge with thin skin?
The dorsum (bridge) can be raised in thin skin pateints with diced cartilage and fascia
Mar 26, 2023
A low dorsum can be augmented using finely diced cartilage. After the cartilage is reconstituted with tissue glue, called Tisseal, we then cover it with perichodrium from the rib where we got the cartilage graft from. This will yield a higher bridge but still have a smooth dorsum even in patients with thin skin.
What are the common medications prescribed right after rhinoplasty (I'm not a fan & take m
Most patients don't need painkillers beyond the night of surgery, and some not even then
Oct 4, 2022
Ever since I stopped using nasal packing after rhinoplasty the level of post op discomfort (don't like to use the word "pain") has gone down significantly. Other medications we prescribe are antibiotics for 24 hours, which is the latest evidence based recommendation, and Arnica Montana, a homeopathic medication to reduce bruising. If you still have pain the day after surgery, studies have also shown that most patients get equal pain relief from a combination of Acetaminophen (Tylenol) and Advil.

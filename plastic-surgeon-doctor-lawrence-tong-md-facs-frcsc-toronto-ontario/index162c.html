Will Skin Continue to Tighten 10 Weeks Post Op Face/necklift?
The neck and facial skin is tightest immediately after surgery
Apr 4, 2011
Hi spokane6975,

At the time of the procedure, the skin is pulled to an appropriate tension. Immediately after surgery (first week) , things are at their tightest due to the swelling. As the swelling resolves (1-4 weeks), the tension will decrease, and the skin will start to relax (1-6 months). As these things occur, you should not expect the neck to tighten; if anything, it will become more loose. Hope this helps.

Best regards,

Lawrence Tong MD FACS FRCSC

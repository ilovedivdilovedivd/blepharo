Eric J. Yavrouian, MD

Name: Eric J. Yavrouian, MD
Statement: Dr. Eric Yavrouian is a double board-certified facial plastic surgeon and otolaryngologist in the Los Angeles area, with over 13 years of experience. While his practice provides a range of surgical and nonsurgical procedures, he specializes in cosmetic and functional rhinoplasty, revision rhinoplasty, and septoplasty. He’s also known for his expertise in facial rejuvenation, including surgical facelifts and blepharoplasty, as well as nonsurgical options such as FaceTite, Botox, and dermal fillers like Juvéderm and Restylane.

“My mission is to provide superb care to my patients, always keeping in mind the fine balance between form and function and tailoring it toward the patient’s desires to ensure a natural result,” says Dr. Yavrouian.

Dr. Yavrouian graduated summa cum laude from the University of California, Los Angeles with a bachelor of science degree in biochemistry. He went on to receive his medical degree from the University of Southern California Keck School of Medicine in Los Angeles. He completed his residency at the University of Southern California with a specialty in otolaryngology – head and neck surgery, as well as a fellowship in facial plastic and reconstructive surgery at the University of Texas Health Science Center in Houston. There, he joined the attending staff as a clinical instructor in facial plastic and reconstructive surgery.

Dr. Yavrouian returned to Los Angeles to establish his practice and recently joined ENT Surgical Associates, which has been serving Glendale, Pasadena, Burbank, and the surrounding communities for more than four decades.

A RealSelf Top Doctor, Dr. Yavrouian frequently lectures on facial plastic surgery and has co-authored articles in several scientific publications, including medical journal articles on advances in biomarkers, surgical techniques for rhinoplasty, and potential targeted therapies in head and neck cancer.

Dr. Yavrouian is board certified by the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery. He is a member of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Academy of Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery.

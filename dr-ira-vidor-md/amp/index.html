Why is my eye swelling/edema under right eye?
Malar edena
Aug 25, 2017
Hi. From your photos, it looks like you have malar edema (cheek swelling). There are many reasons this can happen. Tear trough or cheek filler is a common cause. It can sometimes occur weeks to months after filler injection. I would follow up with your injector to make sure nothing else is going on. If it is just swelling, it typically reaponds very well to a short course of corticosteroids (such as a medrol dosepak). Best regards, Ira Vidor, M.D.

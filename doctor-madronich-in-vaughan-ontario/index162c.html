Is it ok to have permanent eyeliner and Microblading prior to blepharoplasty?
Microblading prior to blepharoplasty
Nov 14, 2018
It is safe to do either microblading or permanent eyeliner prior to your blepharoplasty. You can expect some downtime due to skin redness and darkened pigmentation of the ink during the initial stages of Microblading and permanent eyeliner but should not affect your surgical outcome.

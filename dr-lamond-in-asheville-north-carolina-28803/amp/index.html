What can happen if your lips are overfilled with hyaluronic acid?
Here's What Happens When You Overfill Lips with HA Fillers
Dec 28, 2021
If your lips are overfilled, you have two options. The first, leave it alone and the HA filler will slowly break down and go away over the span of a year or so, depending on the filler and where it was injected.
The other option is to find a practice/provider who is skilled in the use of hyaluronidase, which is an enzyme which can be injected into HA fillers to accelerate the breakdown of the product. It typically works within one to two weeks and is extremely safe and effective.

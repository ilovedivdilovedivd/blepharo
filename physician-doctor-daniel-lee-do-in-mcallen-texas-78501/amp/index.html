Will vaginoplasty leave any visible scars, suture marks after healing?
Vaginoplasty scar
May 13, 2019
As others have commented there will be two suture lines with a vaginoplasty surgery. The inside vagina should heal with no visible scars. The outside perineum incision may lead a very faint scar but in the majority of may patients there is no visible scar.

Had my 5th and final baby 8 weeks ago, what work will be needed with my vagina?
vagina after birth
Apr 22, 2019
It appears that your perineum does have an "indention." I would recommend waiting 6 months after your birth for the scar tissue and inflammation to settle down. It doesn't appear that your vaginal opening is too wide. If anything you may need a perineoplasty to correct the indention. If it's not causing any pain after 6 months then you might not need any type of surgery. Congrats on your birth.

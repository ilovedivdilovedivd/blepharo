How Do I Get Rid of Eye Bags?
Getting rid of Eye Bags
Nov 30, 2017
Eyelid surgery is often the best and most permanent way to get rid of lower lid eye bags, especially if the eye bags are large and prominent. If you have mild eye bags, filling the hollow beneath the eye bags with filler can often camouflage them. Most fillers used around the eyes are not permanent. If loose skin is causing the bags, skin tightening with a laser can also be performed and this is usually a long lasting treatment.

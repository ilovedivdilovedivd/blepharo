Is hair transplant viable if I am 26 (F) with oily scalp and dry hair?
Female hair Transplant
Sep 5, 2019
A female hair transplant is an incredible procedure to enrich the density of the scalp in regions of thinning. If you have a dry scalp, you should work on having this optimized prior to the procedure. This may involve topical (and rarely oral) medicines to improve the scalp. You should see a hair restoration specialist to evaluate you in person and go from there. Best of luck!
Can Minoxidil still work after a laser hair removal treatment?
Minoxidil and beards
Sep 5, 2019
Minoxidil works to stimulate live follicles to grow. If you lasered and destroyed all your beard follicles, you will not see much growth with minoxidil.  Likely, there are some dormant follicles that did not die with the laser...and you will get some sparse growth. I would say that a thick beard is very unlikely given your prior decision to laser the visible hairs. Best of luck deciding whether to try and stimulate a beard or to shave.

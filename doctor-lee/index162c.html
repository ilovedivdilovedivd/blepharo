Statement: Dr Mark Lee is a Specialist Plastic Surgeon based in Perth, Western Australia who’s been in practice for over 19 years. Dr Lee is a partner in the Enhance Plastic Surgical Group, based in Subiaco and also serves the rural and regional community in Albany on the South Coast. He is the current President of the Western Australian Society of Plastic Surgeons and Editor in Chief of the Australasian Journal of Plastic Surgery (AJOPS).

Dr. Lee specializes in cosmetic and reconstructive surgery of the breast, body, and face, including breast augmentation, abdominoplasty, facelifts, and body lifts, as well as blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery) and rhinoplasty. Additionally, he offers a wide range of nonsurgical treatments, including dermal fillers and neuromodulators like Botox, chemical peels, scalp therapy, and laser skin resurfacing.

After graduating medical school from the University of Western Australia in 1993, Dr Lee completed internship and residency at Royal Perth Hospital with rotations through General, Orthopaedic and Cardiac Surgery. Following this he completed a five-year Fellowship Training in Plastic Surgery at Royal Perth Hospital, Sir Charles Gairdner Hospital and Princess Margaret Hospital for Children. He also pursued a fellowship in Microsurgery, Head and Neck Surgery and Cleft Lip and Palate Surgery in Oxford UK at the Radcliffe Infirmary. His time in Oxford also included teaching medical students anatomy at Trinity College , University of Oxford.

Dr. Lee is fellowship certified (Board Certified) by the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons (Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery). He is a member of the Australian Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS) and the Australasian Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ASAPS), as well as a member of the International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgeons (ISAPS).

When Dr. Lee is not helping his patients reach their aesthetic goals, he is educating future generations of plastic surgeons through speaking and authoring numerous papers in the plastic surgery literature. Two of his papers have won Best paper Awards in 2015 and 2021. He has presented papers at multiple international and Australian meetings and authored a book chapter in the Oxford Textbook of Plastic Surgery.

Dr. Lee has helped organize and lead multiple surgical missions to developing countries, where he surgically treats congenital malformations in children who may otherwise not have access to such procedures.
These missions include:
– Tonga and The Solomon Islands in the South Pacific
– Multiple islands in the Philippines
– Provincial cities in Tanzania, Africa
– Svay Rieng, Cambodia
– Gansu province, China
– Amman Jordan
– Agadir Morocco
and most recently Vijayawada in India.

Dr. Lee is proud to have served as an elected councillor for 6 years on the governing board of the Australian Society of Plastic Surgeons. This included a role as treasurer of the society. Whilst on the board he was a founder, and is still Editor in Chief, of The Australasian Journal of Plastic Surgery.

The media routinely taps Dr. Lee for his expertise and insights into the field of plastic surgery. He’s made numerous radio and podcast appearances and has contributed to articles on Plastic Surgery in The West Australian newspaper.

You can schedule a consultation at Dr Lee’s practice in Subiaco or in Albany at The Great Southern Specialist Center.

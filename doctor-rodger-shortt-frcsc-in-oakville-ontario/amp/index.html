Am I a candidate for fat transfer?
Breast fat transfer with a breast lift
Aug 4, 2023
Fat transfer can be a nice adjunct to a breast lift. It can give a bit of a boost, especially in the upper breast and cleavage area. It’s not done by all surgeons. Ideally patients should see someone that does fat transfer regularly. They can go over the pros and cons in detail.
Would a fat transfer hide implants and bony chest?
Breast fat transfer can be used in combination with an implant to provide more natural appearance
Mar 13, 2023
Breast fat transfer can be used in conjunction with a breast implant to provide additional concealment of the edge of the implant as well as the boney prominences of either the ribs or the sternum. This is a fairly nuanced procedure and probably best left to a surgeon that does a fair bit of a fat transfer to the breast.

Everyone needs the opinion of a plastic surgeon in person to go over the procedure, risks and benefits to determine if it is suitable.

Can I get a fat transfer on top of implants?
Increasing an implant is more reliable than adding fat
Oct 30, 2022
It is much more reliable to increase the size of an implant than it is to add fat.

Fat transfer in the setting of an implant can be effective at better concealing the implant. If that is not an issue the simplest thing to increase size, is to just open up the pocket and replace it with a larger implant. Typically when patients exchange implants there is little discomfort. It is a generally straightforward procedure. The value of a plastic surgery consultation cannot be overstated.

Infant with ptosis
Unilateral ptosis left upper lid
Aug 29, 2015
While amblyopia may be associated with ptosis of the upper lid, it is unlikely. Patching will probably note affect the lid height. If there is a question of amblyopia, it is important to treat early. We use the Pediatric Guibor 4-0 Supramid double trapezoidal sling unilaterally in these pediatric cases, if there is any question regarding visual loss. Otherwise, we prefer to wait until 5 years of age and a levator resection works best to correct the blepharoptosis.

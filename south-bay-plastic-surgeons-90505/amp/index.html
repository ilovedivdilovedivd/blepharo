An abdominoplasty with a diastasis repair and liposuction of your waistline (including the back) will achieve a nice hour-glass contour. Breast implants will raise the position of your nipples a bit, but not enough to place them in the center of the breast mound (ideal). So my recommendation for your breasts is a breast lift with or without an implant.

Would I be an ideal candidate for a tummy tuck? 5'7 and 190 lbs
You're a great candidate!
May 31, 2023
If you are otherwise healthy and are unhappy with how your abdomen looks, then yes, you are a great candidate for a tummy tuck. I recommend that my patients are within 10 lbs of their goal weight prior to surgery. You want to avoid losing a lot of weight AFTER your tummy tuck because this may result in more loose skin, so try to be as close to your goal weight as possible prior to surgery.
Can I still have the surgery if my labs are off?
Labs should be normal prior to elective surgery
May 31, 2023
Hopefully your primary care doctor has seen your lab results and will follow up with you to determine why your calcium and other labs are abnormal. Your calcium is only slightly elevated, but can be a sign of problems with your parathyroid glands. High serum calcium can impact your heart function as well.

Implants yes, mini tummy tuck, no
May 31, 2023
You can definitely avoid a breast lift if you have implants because the implants will lift your breasts somewhat. You will have a very natural looking result without a lift. You are not a great candidate for a "mini" tummy tuck though because of the amount of damaged skin you have around and below your belly button. My recommendation would be a full tummy tuck.

Does scar massage really help with the appearance of breast lift scars?
Scar Massage
Nov 2, 2019
Admittedly, the published evidence for scar massage is weak. Having said that, we educate our patients on scar massage in great detail and have seen beautiful results with this. We feel it is a very important part of scar management. Aside from massage, there are several other modalities including silicone scar gel, sheeting and taping. Your surgeon can go over their specific protocol with you.

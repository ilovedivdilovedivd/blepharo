Is a Brazilian butt lift safe after butt implant removal? Do my chances of fat embolism go up due
Staged Approach
Jul 30, 2023
It would be reasonable to remove the implants and then perform a BBL, but this should not be done in one surgery. I would suggest removing the implants first and allowing things several months to settle, and then doing the BBL at a second stage. In your case there will be more scar tissue present, and precautions should be taken when performing a BBL. The safest way to perform a BBL would be under ultrasound guidance to ensure that no fat is placed into the muscle.

Can I use nose strips 11 months after rhinoplasty?
The nose heals for one full year after rhinoplasty
Aug 3, 2015
It is important to see your surgeon regularly after rhinoplasty, the nose continues to heal and change. It is important to see the surgeon and document changes. If the nose is well healed I do not oppose using nasal strips. The surgeon may also want to examine the septum to make sure there are no structural problems that need to be addressed to avoid nasal obstruction.
Can a Rhinoplasty achieve biracial harmony?
Blending nasal esthetics
Mar 3, 2015
Balance, harmony and proportion are common to all beautiful noses regardless of race. The experienced rhinoplasty surgeon takes into account race, face symmetry to achieve harmonious results.

Dermal fillers may be an option
Mar 3, 2015
Dermal fillers are a great option for some rhytids. Also laser can be of benefit. It is best to consult a physician to see what best suits your needs.
Am I a candidate for a non surgical nose job? I live in Toronto
Non surgical rhinoplasty
Mar 3, 2015
Non surgical rhinoplasty can improve a variety of nasal deformities and improve balance in the nose. This technique relies on fillers and can be very effective in making very subtle adjustments to the nose.
Will non surgical rhinoplasty be an option to fix the bump on my nose?
Surgical vs Non Surgical Rhinoplasty
Mar 3, 2015
Non surgical rhinoplasty is faster, more cost effective and does not require general anesthetic. However, the procedure cannot remove a hump. Some “effacing” of the hump may occur. It is best to explore all options with a surgeon who performs both procedures.
Is something weird? Is the asymmetry pronounced?
Facial asymmetry is normal
Feb 26, 2015
A slight bit of asymmetry is normal in a face and can make it look more real. When most faces are carefully analyzed there is some asymmetry.
1 months post op, is it normal for my nose to block after Septoplasty?
Post op nasal blockage
Feb 26, 2015
Post nasal surgery some bleeding and blockage can occur. Nasal rinses and good post op care are often all that is required and over time the nose heals.

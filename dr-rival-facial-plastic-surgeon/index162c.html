My nose tip points down 10 months after surgery. Help! What are my options?
Tip support is needed
Nov 20, 2019
It appears your nasal tip has lost support after your surgery and dropped. Sometimes this happens in a strong projected nose, after reducing it. Revision surgery by an experienced rhinoplasty surgeon should give you a much improved result.

Natural looking rhinoplasty
Oct 27, 2019
Yes I think the changes you’ve made with the app are quite realistic. I think you would be a good candidate for rhinoplasty. I would also lift and set back the tip and refine it along with reducing the bridge. Make sure you go to someone who is very experienced and specializes in rhinoplasty.
Bulbous Tip – What are the challenges and realistic results?
Nasal tip refinement
Feb 10, 2018
You appear to be a good candidate for rhinoplasty to narrow and define your tip. During the procedure we reduce and reshape the cartilages but we cant change the skin. The final result depends on how the overlying skin and soft tissue contracts after surgery. Thicker skin tends not to contract and may hide the work that has been done to the cartilages. You appear to have large cartilages and thin skin so I would expect significant improvement. Hope this helps!

I think you would be good candidate for rhinoplasty. Alar rim grafts cause minimal swelling
Apr 16, 2017

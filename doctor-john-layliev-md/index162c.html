ear numbness after facelift
Apr 3, 2019
Earlobe numbness 6 weeks after a facelift is not uncommon. Most commonly, swelling around the great auricular nerve is the cause of this or a stretch of the tissue around the nerve. If your facial expressions and smile is symmetric and normal, then there has not been any injury to the motor branches of the facial nerve- this is a feared and very rare complication that would be apparent after surgery.

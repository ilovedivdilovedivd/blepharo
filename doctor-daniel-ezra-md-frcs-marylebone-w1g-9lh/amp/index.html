Is it possible to have upper eyelid surgery with SVTs?
No Adrenaline, No Problem.
Jan 12, 2020
I am extremely surprised that yo have been turned down for surgery. It is actually very common for people to ask to have this surgery without the use of adrenaline. It is perfect possible to apply local anaesthesia without adrenaline. For the purposes of upper eyelid surgery, this will not impact significantly on bleeding. I would normally ask for cardiology opinion before surgery.Best of luck for your future treatment.
Do I need more skin removal after ptosis surgery four years ago?
Skin crease asymmetry
Dec 1, 2019
There is asymmetry in the skin creases which creates too much tarsal show, making the lid look fat. This is possible to correct with a skin crease reformation. My own feeling is that it looks fine and the issue is only a mild one. 
Botox for acquired synkinesis, but what about congenital synkinesis?
Its a different kind of synkenesis
Nov 29, 2019
Marcus-Gunn is a very difficult condition to manage. The reason that botox does not work is because the synkinetic movement induces inhibition and overaction of the elevator muscle. botox here will simply cause a severe ptosis.Surgical Treatments for Marcus Gan be effective and centre around correcting underlying ptosis, and in some cases transecting the elevator and holding the lid open with a brow suspension system.

What can I do to my eyes?
Various options
Oct 15, 2019
There is some significant asymmetry due to different skin creases and a small right ptosis. I would suggest a ptosis correction with skin crease reformation, and address the lower lids with tear trough/cheek filler in the first instance. You should get a very good improvement from this as a first step.
Why are my eyes still swollen?
Wrong filler used
May 27, 2019
Im not sure which type of Revolax was used, but these are more cohesive fillers that can cause long-term swelling. At this point, you will need to have the fillers dissolved and a more gentle formulation injected as the tear troughs are a very sensitive area.

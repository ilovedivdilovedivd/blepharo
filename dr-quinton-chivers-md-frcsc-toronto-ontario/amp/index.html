I’ve been using Latisse for 40 days, and can’t see any results. Is it normal?
How long does it take Latisse to work
Jul 3, 2016
Hair regrowth is not instant-  Similar to hair transplantation and with all hair regrowth products it takes a period of time for medications/surgery to take effect.  In the case of Latisse full product effectiveness may not be seen for up to 2 months.   In the case of hair transplantation this can be up to 6 months as the hair initially goes into a dormancy period.  Be patient and continue application for the recommended duration.Warm Regards,Dr Quinton ChiversPlastic and Reconstructive Surgery

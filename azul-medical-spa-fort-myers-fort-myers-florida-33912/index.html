Doctors

Patrick Flaharty, MD FAACS

Name: Patrick Flaharty, MD FAACS
Statement: Dr. Patrick Flaharty is a board-certified oculoplastic surgeon and the medical director of Azul Cosmetic Surgery Medical Spa, with locations in Bonita Springs, Naples, and Fort Myers, Florida.

Trained as an ophthalmologist and facial cosmetic surgeon, Dr. Flaharty specializes exclusively in procedures for the face, neck, and delicate tissues of the eyes, including eyelid surgery, facelift, brow lift, neck lift, laser skin rejuvenation, and Botox and dermal filler injections. Throughout nearly 30 years in practice, he has performed over 20,000 facial plastic and cosmetic procedures.

Dr. Flaharty received his medical degree from the University of Michigan Medical School in Ann Arbor and completed his ophthalmology residency at the prestigious Wills Eye Hospital in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He continued his training with an ophthalmic and facial plastic surgery fellowship at the University of Utah in Salt Lake City and a facial cosmetic surgery fellowship in Orlando, Florida.

An in-demand speaker at medical summits and for the news media, Dr. Flaharty routinely presents at national aesthetic meetings, including the Las Vegas Cosmetic Surgery Conference and the Global Aesthetic Conference. He also hosts Look & Feel Your Best, a weekly radio program, and has written two books, Look Younger Now and Young Again, on the topic of facial aging and rejuvenation.

Board-certified by the American Board of Ophthalmology, Dr. Flaharty is a member of the American Society of Ophthalmic Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (ASOPRS).

Fat graft or removal?
Fat Grafting versus Fillers
May 27, 2022
For the small contour irregularities in your photographs fillers are by far the best option. Fillers are an easy, non-surgical options that is adjustable and reversible and perfect to address these small contour changes that occur with age. Fat grafting is much less predictable and forgiving and therefore not a practical way to address these changes. Consulting with a physician experienced in facial fillers is the best way to learn more about this option.

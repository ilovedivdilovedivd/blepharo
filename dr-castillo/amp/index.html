How Long Before Botox Results Can Be Seen?
Be patient
Feb 20, 2018
It can be up to 10 days before you see full results from a Botox treatment. Be aware that one side may react sooner than the other, so if you see uneven results at 6 days, give it 4 more days before you consider a touch-up!
Is Minoxidil for Short-term or Long-term Use?
Must continue use
Jun 1, 2017
Minoxidil must be used daily, and for as long as the user wants to see improvement with hair growth. It takes at least 3-6 months of consistent, proper application to begin to see hair growth. If a week is skipped, patients will report seeing a decrease almost immediately. I wouldn’t recommend starting it if you don’t intend to continue using it indefinitely!

ThermiVa: How Long Do The Results Last?
ThermiVa results last for generally a year, then maintenance is recommended
Jun 1, 2017
From studies thus far, the results of ThermiVa will generally last at least a year, then maintenance is recommended every 12 to 18 months. It should be noted that initial treatments consist of a series of 3 treatments, but maintenance is usually only one treatment. At our facility, the maintenance treatment is greatly discounted to encourage women to maintain their results.

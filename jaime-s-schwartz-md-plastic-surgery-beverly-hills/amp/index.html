Brazilian butt lift. I'm 34 with hypothyroidism. Do you think this surgery is pointless?
Brazilian Butt Lift
Mar 29, 2019
A good candidate has enough fat available for transfer in the procedure. The amount of fat that can be transferred in a BBL depends on how much fat the patient has to harvest and the amount of space there is to move the fat into. It is recommended for the patient to have a BMI of 30 or less. If you are too thin, a Brazilian Butt Lift may not be possible.

I am wanting a BBL and tummy tuck. Would I do better with a mommy makeover?
Brazilian Butt Lift
Mar 28, 2019
The best way to achieve a celebrity-like butt and hips is a Brazilian Butt Lift. A Brazilian Butt Lift is a procedure where excess fat is liposuctioned from different parts of the body and moved to the buttocks. This reshapes and adds volume to the butt while keeping a natural look. Final results are permanent and generally seen about 6 months after the procedure.

What kind of medical procedure can fix my acne scarring between my eyebrows?
Scar treatments
Aug 29, 2019
Scars can be treated many ways depending on the location, your skin type, type of scar, etc. For severely depressed scars (indented) it is sometimes best to have a surgical revision. Other options include sub-incision with or without filler or fat injections over a few sessions. Fractional lasers and microneedling with or without platelet rich plasma can also help with skin and scar remodeling. Often a combination of approaches can give you the best outcome. Good luck!

Rhinoplasty/Nose Surgery Costs

A primary rhinoplasty price range is typically from $5,000 to $10,000. Determining factors are complexity, surgeon, facility, and anesthesia. Be sure to have a consult with a board certified surgeon.
I was wondering if I'm being overcharged for a mini lift, does $8900 sound right?
Is $8900 a reasonable price for a mini facelift?
Oct 24, 2019
Pricing will vary nationally depending on the location of the facility. Be sure you are clear on what is included in the price...surgeon, facility, anesthesia, pre/post op appointments. Also, choose a Board Certified surgeon with experience and expertise. $8900 is in the ballpark for a mini facelift.
Where are the incisions for a mini-lift made?
For a mini facelift, where are the incisions located?
Oct 24, 2019
As no facelift is a "cookie-cutter" procedure-with a mini facelift, incisions would usually be in front of the ear and around the lobe to behind the ear to ensure the SMAS muscle and skin is tightened. These incisions can be hidden in the contour (crease in front) of the ear so once healed they are almost invisible.

That being said, I would first recommend achieving some improvement with Ultherapy and/or InstaLift. I think you would be more satisfied with a full facelift down the road to tighten neck and jowls.

Steroids to reduce swelling after facelift? My doctor said they can have serious side effects. What
Using steroids to reduce swelling after facelift.
Oct 14, 2019
For many patients, a preoperative prescription of a Medrol Dosepak for swelling after facelift surgery is fine. It is important to start taking it prior to surgery to achieve the greatest benefit. Serious side effects are rare. The most reported side effect from Medrol in my practice is insomnia. Definitely discuss your concerns with your surgeon.
How Do I Minimize Bruising from a Rhinoplasty?
Tips to minimize bruising after a rhinoplasty...
Oct 10, 2019
Be proactive by discontinuing aspirin, ibuprofen,vitamin E, herbal supplements and any medication that thins the blood. Begin taking arnica montana and bromelain supplements a minimum of 1 week prior to surgery. Post surgery be sure to keep your head elevated and apply ice as instructed. Avoid any type of strenuous activity that would increase blood flow to the area for at least 2 weeks.

The truth is that there isn't a "best" age to get a facelift. A person in their forties can have the same laxity in the face as a person in their sixties. Factors such as lifestyle (smoking, sun exposure) and genetics play a large role in the aging process. "I wish I wouldn't have waited so long" is a common statement in our practice. How Soon After Rhinoplasty Can I Fly or Exercise?
Wait 1-2 weeks prior to flying or exercising-possibly longer depending on recovery.
Oct 9, 2019
Flying can cause sinus pressure discomfort and possible bleeding. Exercising increases blood flow and can cause swelling. Best to wait 1-2 weeks before flying or exercising.
Sleeping Position After Rhinoplasty?
A recliner is the best Post Rhinoplasty sleeping position to help reduce swelling.
Oct 9, 2019
Sleeping at a 30-45 degree angle in a recliner is ideal. Another option is using pillows under the head while in bed to achieve this angle, and adding a pillow under the knees to make it less likely to slide down.

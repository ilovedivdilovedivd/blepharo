EVLT, phlebectomy under general anesthesia?
general anesthesia is not required
Mar 21, 2023
You definitely do not need general anesthesia for these procedures. Both can be done in the office setting with local (tumescent) anesthesia, and are very straightforward procedures.
Unfortunately, if the surgeon is employed by a hospital, they can coerce the surgeon to bring you to the operating room so the hospital can make more money off of the procedure. I’ve seen it happen before.
What method of lipo is best for athletic body? Vaserlipo or power assisted lipo?
both are excellent
Mar 21, 2023
They are not mutually exclusive procedures, and can certainly be done during the same procedure. The Vaser essentially melts the fat smoothly and evenly, while power assisted liposuction then sucks it out. Both are good options, but neither are a replacement for an experienced body contouring surgeon.

Asymmetric Eyelids, Completely Different Shapes - Is There Any Solution?
Eyelid asymmetry can be improved
Oct 25, 2012
I can't tell much from the darker photos but from the B/W photo, it appears you have a slight asymmetry in the left upper lid caused by less fat in this lid. You are in upgaze and it emphasizes the hollowing and asymmetry. I have usually treated this with a small amount of filler like Restylane in the upper lid with excellent results. There are some risks with the injection and an experienced injector of the lids can convey these to you. Good luck.
Do I Need a Prescription for the Eye Drops That Help with Droopy Eyelids After Botox?
Drops for droops
Oct 18, 2011
Yes you need a prescription. You can use something less expensive than Iopidine. You can have your doctor write for a prescription of 1.25% phenylephrine drops (the usual concentration is 2.5%). These are the same drops eye doctors use for dilating eyes but at a low concentration, they will elevate the lid with minimal dilation of the pupil.
Can I Get Almond Shaped Eyes Without Having Surgery?
Genetics
Oct 13, 2011

Although many of these companies do sell legitimate Botox, the FDA has no control over those substances that have been imported by a physician. Can Cosmetic Eye Surgery Be Advised for a Droopy Eyelid Patient Who Had Cataract Surgery ?
Eyelid surgery after cataract surgery
Aug 12, 2011
You can have cosmetic lid surgery after cataract surgery as long as you have healed from the surgery. Check with your cataract surgeon to determine when it is safe to proceed. There should be no problems in doing the eyelid surgery. Good luck!

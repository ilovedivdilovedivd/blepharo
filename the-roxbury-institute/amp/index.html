David Amron, MD

Name: David Amron, MD
Statement: Dr. David Amron is a board-certified dermatologic surgeon based in Beverly Hills, California. As founder of The Roxbury Institute and the Advanced Lipedema Treatment Program, Dr. Amron specializes in lipedema treatment, cosmetic and revision liposuction and body contouring procedures. He has refined his technique and artistry over close to three decades and has performed over 5,000 lipedema surgeries and 10,000 liposuction procedures—a depth of experience that has made him one of the most in-demand liposuction surgeons in the Los Angeles area.

Dr. Amron’s guiding philosophy is about bringing balance to each patient’s proportions and creating a refined silhouette. He takes an all-encompassing, holistic approach in assessing a patient’s unique anatomy, needs, and goals. During a consultation, Dr. Amron evaluates the entire body with three factors in mind: weight, disproportion, and laxity. Looking at the body in its entirety and developing a customized body sculpting plan results in more natural-looking outcomes.

After graduating from the University of California San Diego with a degree in Biology, Dr. Amron attended the Albert Einstein College of Medicine in New York, where he obtained his medical degree. He then returned to UCSD to complete his residency. A research fellowship at UCLA followed.

Dr. Amron is board-certified by the American Board of Dermatology and a member of numerous medical societies including the American Academy of Cosmetic Surgery, the American Society for Liposuction Surgery, and the American Society for Dermatologic Surgery.

A leader in his field, Dr. Amron has contributed to a number of medical journals and authored chapters on dermatologic surgery in medical textbooks. He has been featured in a variety of TV and news outlets, including The Doctors, The Today Show, The Discovery Channel, Good Morning America, the Wall Street Journal, Cosmopolitan, and the LA Times.

Tattoo after Coolsculpting
Nov 9, 2020
There is not a contraindication to get a tattoo in the area that coolsculpting was performed, however, I would probably wait about 3 months before getting the tattoo in case there is residual swelling so that it does not alter the shape of the tattoo once area is fully healed.

I accidentally used a face wash that had Vitamin C and glycerin a day after microneedling. Should I
Post Microneedling
Sep 18, 2020
Glycerin is a humectant which means it pulls water into the skin and keeps it hydrated. For that reason, this is actually a great ingredient for post procedure skin. Vitamin C is safe and effective vs UV rays and sun damage, however, can sometimes be very burny on freshly Microneedled skin. If it feels too hot and burny, I would rinse the product off your face with cool water and wait a couple days before you resume using that face wash again.

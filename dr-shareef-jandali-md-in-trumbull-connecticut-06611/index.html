Breast augmentation and areola reduction
Jan 9, 2020
If you just undergo a breast augmentation, many patients think that since their entire breast will be larger, then their areolae will look smaller. However, the areola skin will stretch just like the rest of the breast skin after augmentation. Your best option would likely be a breast augmentation combined with an areola reduction at the same time. Please consult with a board certified plastic surgeon.

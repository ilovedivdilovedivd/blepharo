Can Tretinoin and Azelaic Acid be used simultaneously?
Combination of tretinoin and azelaic acid for oily skin
Oct 24, 2018
I do not use that combination of treatments often, but I will recommend them if the person has severely oily skin. Combined with a good moisturizer they can be effective if the person is not to sensitive and starts slowly using the tretinoin every other night for one to two weeks then escalate to daily use.

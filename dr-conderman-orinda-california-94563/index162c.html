Is it safe to have a rhinoplasty done at the age of 48?
Rhinoplasty Age 48
Nov 14, 2023
Hi,
While the structural framework of the nose does change with time, I have had plenty of patients get nasal surgery at your age. I would recommend a full assessment with evaluation of the internal and external aspects of your nose by someone that does a lot of nasal surgery. As we age, there are additional technical considerations but age alone should not prevent you from having nasal surgery. Hope that helps!

Rhinoplasty Recovery
Nov 14, 2023
Hi!
I have had patients go back to work as early as 1-2 days after an open rhinoplasty.

Would a lower blepharoplasty help my bags?
Blepharoplasty Candidate
Oct 2, 2023
Yes you are a good candidate for lower blepharoplasty. The technique I would use in your case would be transconjunctival (hidden incision) with lower lid fat repositioning or fat grafting.
Rhinoplasty for a round nose?
Candidate for Rhinoplasty?
Sep 28, 2023
You are a good candidate for rhinoplasty. The nasal tip can be narrowed and more defined with rhinoplasty. Your profile is very nice but you have a slight dorsal hump which can be reduced and make it look even better.
Worried rhinoplasty could worsen the bulbous tip because I get keloid scarring, is it a good idea
Rhinoplasty for Bulbous Nasal Tip

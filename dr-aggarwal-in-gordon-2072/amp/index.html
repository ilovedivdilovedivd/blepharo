My right eye is higher than my left. What can I do?
In short – no
Nov 11, 2018
Hi there – normal asymmetries like yours are so common that they are generally the rule rather than the exception. The only surgery that exists is an orbital box osteotomy which is performed by plastic surgeons that specialise in craniofacial surgery – this is usually reserved for kids or adolescents with severe craniofacial deformity or syndromes which necessitates this huge procedure. In your case the risks would outweigh the benefits.
Good question though!

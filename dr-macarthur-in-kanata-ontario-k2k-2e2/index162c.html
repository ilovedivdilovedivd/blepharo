Do I have ptosis or hooded eyes? What can I do to enhance them? 32 y/o
Ptosis
Dec 17, 2022
Thank you for your question. It is not possible to make a diagnosis of eyelid ptosis based on these photos. Ptosis refers to the margin of the upper eyelid being too low in relation to the iris and pupil. I suggest you schedule an appointment with a board-certified plastic surgeon or ophthalmologist in order to be investigated for this condition. Take care.
Is it best to get TKR before mommy makeover?
Knee replacement
Dec 17, 2022
Thanks for your question. I suggest having the knee replacement done first before your mommy makeover. It is very important for patients to ambulate (get up and walk) after having a tummy tuck in order to prevent a blood clot from developing. If you aren't able to walk well before surgery you may have a higher risk of developing this complication. Take care.

Columella

Nerves need time to heal too
Jul 13, 2015
It sounds like you are going through a fairly normal period of healing to me. Remember that when you have implants placed the tissues need to stretch to accommodate the new implant. This includes the nerves that have now been stretched over the implant. As the nerves recover, your symptoms should get better but this can take a few weeks.
Could I have implants without a lift?
No Lift Needed
Jul 12, 2015
From your pictures it looks as though your nipples lie well above the inframammary fold. In these circumstances I would not perform a lift. If you have some skin on skin contact below the breast then you may do better with a subglandular lift where the implant lies above the muscle.

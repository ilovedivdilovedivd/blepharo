Are the following multiple procedures safe?
Multiple Surgeries
Jun 7, 2020
Hi, Generally it is safe to do multiple surgeries at the single operation, as long as you are healthy and each component part of the surgery isn't too extensive. We often combine breast and abdominal surgery like this. You would need a consult to advise what surgery is needed, how long each part would take and based on this, if they can be done together.Thanks for your enquiry,Dr Steve Merten, Sydney, Australia

Implant Shape
Aug 27, 2019

If they have been getting fuller, you may have a fluid collection around the implants. I recommend you return to your original surgeon to check there is no problem.Regards, Dr Steve Merten, Sydney

Lower body lift vs FDL abdominoplasty
Aug 14, 2019
Hi, part of your decision should be what you need around the sides and buttock area. A lower body lift will address these areas, whereas an abdominoplasty (even with the vertical FDL excision) will not.I usually do a lower body lift, which usually does allow adequate tightening of the upper abdomen and improves the lateral abdomen, lateral upper thighs and buttocks. If there is still some upper abdominal looseness, a later vertical excision can always be done, but is rarely necessary....

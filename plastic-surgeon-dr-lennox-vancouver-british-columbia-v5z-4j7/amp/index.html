BA. 750cc Saline, High Profile, Under Muscle? I’m 5 feet, 105 lbs with three kids, and have saggy b
Large Implants
Nov 7, 2015
Thank you for your question. We know that as you increase implant size (over around 350cc) the complication rate goes up, and the predictability of the result goes down. Also, with large saline implants, the chances of your tissues supporting the implant in the position you want over the long term is low. Finally, increasing implant size does not lift the breast. You should get some other opinions from board certified plastic surgeons with experience in breast surgery.

New Implants

What can cause replacement implants to appear smaller?
Implant profile
May 14, 2015
The profile of the implant can also affect this. For example, lower profile implants are wider, but less projecting. If your original saline implants were higher profile, they would project more and may appear larger to you, whereas a wider low profile (higher volume) gel implant may fill your bra less.
Wondering if the shape of the implant is the issue with rippling?
Implant rippling
May 14, 2015
Shaped implants are textured, and are supposed to adhere to your tissue. When they do this in a thin person with minimal soft tissue coverage, you can get “traction rippling”, which you have. Solutions include changing to a submuscular plane (it looks like your implants are above the muscle), potentially changing to smooth implants, and possibly also fat grafting.You should obviously return to your original surgeon and review your concerns.
6 weeks post op (capsulectomy). Bubble, pain, swelling?
Post Op
May 14, 2015
Thanks for the question. At 6 weeks post op it is often difficult to assess things due to swelling etc. You should see your surgeon who knows you and can give you a better assessment. Also, depending on how long you had capsular contracture on the left side, the soft tissues on that side may take awhile to relax and be more like the side that is soft and mobile.

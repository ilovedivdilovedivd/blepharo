Facelift Type

Is a corticosteroid nasal spray safe to use prior to rhinoplasty?
Flonase prior to Rhinoplasty
Oct 4, 2023
Great question and every surgeon has somewhat varying opinions on exactly when to stop steroid nasal sprays prior to surgery. Typically, I recommend stopping these 1 month prior to surgery to avoid nasal dryness and bleeding during and after surgery. Flonase can be taken daily for years without any systemic side effects so continuous use is reasonable for treatment of allergies otherwise.

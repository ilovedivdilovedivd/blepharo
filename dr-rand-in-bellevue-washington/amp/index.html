Statement: Dr. Richard P. Rand is a board-certified plastic surgeon and founder of Northwest Center for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery in Bellevue, Washington. While he has expertise in a broad range of procedures for the face and body, Dr. Rand is particularly well-known for his precision and excellence in breast augmentation and lifts, tummy tucks (abdominoplasty), and complete mommy makeovers, as well as facelifts, eyelid surgery, and neck lifts.

“Since beginning my plastic surgery career 30-plus years ago, my staff and I have been entirely focused on providing the kind of care, attention to detail, and superior results that we would want for ourselves and our families,” says Dr. Rand. “This overriding dedication drives every decision we make and guides us every day to be our best.”

Dr. Rand earned his undergraduate degree from Stanford University and his medical degree from the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. In his postgraduate studies, he completed two residencies: one in General Surgery at Tufts Medical Center in Boston, and a second in Plastic Surgery at Emory University in Atlanta. He also completed a fellowship in Craniofacial Surgery at Miami University in Ohio.

Today, Dr. Rand is double board certified by both the American Board of Surgery and the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He is also a member of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (AAFPRS), the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ASAPS), and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS), as well as a fellow of the American College of Surgeons.

Throughout his career as a plastic surgeon serving the greater Seattle area, Dr. Rand has received more than 60 Best Doctors awards from local magazines and national organizations and websites.

What is Recovery Time on Lower Facelift?
Recovery after a facelift
Apr 26, 2019
Most patients resume everyday activities in terms of driving, starting to get around at 10 days. You will still have some bruising and swelling at the stage, however, but this can usually be disguised by makeup and scarves. In terms of working allow 2-3 weeks off for face-to-face contact, but most people can resume desk work at home after one week. Most patients feel completely normal and return to exercise at about 3 weeks.

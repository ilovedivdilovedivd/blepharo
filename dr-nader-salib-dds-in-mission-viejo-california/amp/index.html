I had all my teeth removed about 8 years ago and wanted to know if its to late have implants done
Teeth loss leads to bone loss
Apr 16, 2016
Excellent question. Our teeth help to keep bon by stimulating it. Whenever you chew, the vibrations from the crown travel to the roots and to the surrounding bone stimulating it, keeping it alive.  When teeth are removed, your bone will shrink. To determine if you’re eligible for implants, a proper clinical and radiographic exam should be performed. A CBCT (come beam CT) will allow you to visualize your available bone and even help plan implant placement!
